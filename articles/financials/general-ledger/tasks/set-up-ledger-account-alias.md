# <a name="set-up-a-ledger-account-alias"></a><span data-ttu-id="413a1-103">Konfigurere et finanskontoalias</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="413a1-103">Set up a ledger account alias</span></span>

[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="413a1-104">I denne procedure vises det, hvordan du opretter et kontoalias, som giver en genvej til angivelse af et kontonummer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="413a1-104">This procedure shows how to create an account alias that provides a shortcut for entering an account number.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="413a1-105">Denne procedure bruger demodatafirmaet USMF.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="413a1-105">This procedure users demo data company USMF.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="413a1-106">Gå til Finans > Kontoplan > Konti > Finanskontoalias.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="413a1-106">Go to General ledger > Chart of accounts > Accounts > Ledger account alias.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="413a1-107">Klik på Ny.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="413a1-107">Click New.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="413a1-108">Skriv en værdi i feltet Finanskontoalias.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="413a1-108">In the Ledger account alias field, type a value.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="413a1-109">I feltet Kontostruktur skal du vælge den struktur, som kontoen og dimensionerne tilhører.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="413a1-109">In the Account structure field, select the structure the account and dimensions belong to.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="413a1-110">Klik på rullelisten i feltet Firma for at åbne opslaget.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="413a1-110">In the Company field, click the drop-down button to open the lookup.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="413a1-111">Søg på listen, og vælg det regnskab, som aliaset gælder for.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="413a1-111">In the list, find and select the company that the alias applies to.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="413a1-112">Klik op linket i den valgte række på listen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="413a1-112">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="413a1-113">Angiv kontoen og dimensionerne i feltet Angivelse af finanskontoalias.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="413a1-113">In the Ledger account alias definition field, specify the account and dimensions.</span></span>
    * <span data-ttu-id="413a1-114">Den kontoen og dimensionerne udfyldes, når du bruger genvejen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="413a1-114">The account and dimensions will be populated when using the shortcut.</span></span>  
9. <span data-ttu-id="413a1-115">I feltet Startfokusering skal du vælge den dimension, der har fokus, når aliaset bruges.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="413a1-115">In the Initial focus field, select the dimension that will have focus when the alias is used.</span></span>
    * <span data-ttu-id="413a1-116">Når du har indtastet genvejen, og kontoen og dimensionerne er udfyldt, flyttes markøren eller fokus til feltet Startfokusering.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="413a1-116">After you type the shortcut, and the account and dimensions are populated, the Initial focus field is where the cursor or focus will move to.</span></span>
