<span data-ttu-id="4f49d-105">EU-direktivet indeholder bestemmelser, der skal følges af alle medlemsstater, med hensyn til faktureringsregler og oplysninger, der skal medtages, når der oprettes fakturaer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4f49d-105">EU Directive provides regulations to be followed by all Member States regarding invoice rules and information that must be included when creating invoices.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4f49d-106">EU-direktivet indeholder de oplysninger, der kræves af leverandører for deres kunder i EU.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4f49d-106">EU Directive lists the information that is required by suppliers for their customers in the EU.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4f49d-107">F.eks. kan en faktura indeholde en reference, der beskriver de juridiske grunde til en momssats på nul eller en momsundtagelse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4f49d-107">For example, an invoice could include a reference that describes the legal grounds for a VAT zero-rating or an exemption.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4f49d-108">Hvis en faktura justerer en tidligere udstedt faktura, f.eks. en kreditnota, skal der findes en utvetydig reference til den oprindelige faktura.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4f49d-108">If an invoice adjusts an earlier issued invoice, such as a credit note, an unambiguous reference should be made to the original invoice.</span></span>
