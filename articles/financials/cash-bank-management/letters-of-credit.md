[!INCLUDE [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="a3bf5-105">Remburser bruges i internationale transaktioner til at sikre betalinger.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a3bf5-105">Letters of credit are used for international transactions to ensure that payments will be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a3bf5-106">En remburs er en aftale, der udfærdiges af en bank, hvor banken accepterer at sikre betaling på vegne af køberen, hvis vilkårene i aftalen mellem køberen og sælgeren overholdes.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a3bf5-106">A letter of credit is an agreement that is issued by a bank, in which the bank agrees to ensure payment on behalf of the buyer, if the terms of the agreement between the buyer and seller are met.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a3bf5-107">Bemærk, at en remburs også kaldes et akkreditiv.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a3bf5-107">Note that a letter of credit is also referred to as a documentary credit (DC).</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="a3bf5-108">I en importremburs er den juridiske enhed køberen eller ansøgeren til rembursen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a3bf5-108">For an import letter of credit, the legal entity is the buyer or the applicant for the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a3bf5-109">I en eksportremburs er den juridiske enhed sælgeren eller beneficianten af rembursen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a3bf5-109">For an export letter of credit, the legal entity is the seller or the beneficiary of the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="a3bf5-110">Følgende parter er involveret i en kreditnota:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a3bf5-110">The following parties are involved with a letter of credit:</span></span> 

 - <span data-ttu-id="a3bf5-111">Rekvirenten (køber), som har til hensigt at betale for varerne</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a3bf5-111">The applicant (buyer) who intends to pay for the goods</span></span> 
 - <span data-ttu-id="a3bf5-112">Beneficianten (sælger), som skal modtage betalingen</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a3bf5-112">The beneficiary (seller) who will receive the payment</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="a3bf5-113">Den udstedende bank, der udsteder rembursen</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a3bf5-113">The issuing bank that issues the letter of credit</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="a3bf5-114">Den adviserende bank, der udfører transaktionen på vegne af rekvirenten</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="a3bf5-114">The advising bank that carries out the transaction on behalf of the applicant</span></span>
