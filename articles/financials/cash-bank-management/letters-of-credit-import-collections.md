<a name="letters-of-credit"></a><span data-ttu-id="35f44-107">Remburser</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-107">Letters of credit</span></span>
-----------------

<span data-ttu-id="35f44-108">Remburser bruges i internationale transaktioner og er med til at garantere sikre betalinger.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-108">Letters of credit are used for international transactions and help guarantee that payments will be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35f44-109">En remburs er en aftale, der udfærdiges af en bank, hvor banken accepterer at garantere betaling på vegne af en køber forudsat, at vilkårene i aftalen mellem køberen og sælgeren overholdes.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-109">A letter of credit is an agreement that is issued by a bank, in which the bank agrees to guarantee payment on behalf of a buyer, provided that the terms of the agreement between the buyer and seller are met.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35f44-110">En remburs også kaldes et akkreditiv.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-110">A letter of credit is also referred to as a documentary credit (DC).</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="35f44-111">I en importremburs er den juridiske enhed køberen eller ansøgeren til rembursen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-111">For an import letter of credit, the legal entity is the buyer or the applicant for the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35f44-112">I en eksportremburs er den juridiske enhed sælgeren eller beneficianten af rembursen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-112">For an export letter of credit, the legal entity is the seller or the beneficiary of the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35f44-113">Følgende parter er involveret i en remburs:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-113">The following parties are involved in a letter of credit:</span></span>

-   <span data-ttu-id="35f44-115">Beneficianten (sælger), som skal modtage betalingen</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-115">The beneficiary (seller) who will receive the payment</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="35f44-116">Den udstedende bank, der udsteder rembursen</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-116">The issuing bank that issues the letter of credit</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="35f44-118">Rembursen indeholder en beskrivelse af varerne, evt. nødvendige dokumenter, afsendelsesdatoen og udløbsdatoen, efter hvilken betaling ikke vil blive foretaget.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-118">The letter of credit includes a description of the goods, any required documents, the date of shipment, and the expiration date after which payment won't be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35f44-119">Den udstedende bank opkræver et depositum for rembursen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-119">The issuing bank collects a margin for the letter of credit.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="35f44-120">En remburs kan enten være **genkaldelig** eller **uigenkaldelig**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-120">A letter of credit can be **revocable** or **irrevocable**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35f44-121">Typen af en remburs kan være **overdragelig**, **uoverdragelig** eller **revolverende**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-121">The nature of a letter of credit can be **transferable**, **non-transferable**, or **revolving**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35f44-122">En remburs er typisk en uigenkaldelig og bekræftet aftale om, at betaling vil blive foretaget til en bestemt beneficiant ved fremsendelse af fuldstændig og korrekt forsendelsesdokumentation.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-122">Typically, a letter of credit is an irrevocable and confirmed agreement that payment will be made to a specific beneficiary upon submission of complete and accurate shipping documentation.</span></span>

## <a name="import-collections"></a><span data-ttu-id="35f44-123">Importinkassoer</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-123">Import collections</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="35f44-124">En importinkasso er en aftale mellem banken og eksportøren (sælger), hvor banken accepterer at levere forsendelsesdokumentationen til den internationale importør (køber).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-124">An import collection is an agreement between the bank and the exporter (seller), in which the bank agrees to deliver the shipping documentation to the international importer (buyer).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35f44-125">Banken forventes at levere forsendelsesdokumentationen ved modtagelse af betaling for de sendte varer i form af kontanter eller ved modtagelse af en underskrevet veksel til dækning af betalingen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-125">The bank is expected to deliver the shipping documentation upon receipt of payment for the shipped goods in cash, or upon receipt of a signed draft toward the payment.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="35f44-126">En importinkasso er med til at garantere, at sælger betales, når køberen afhenter forsendelsesdokumenterne for at tage imod levering af de importerede varer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35f44-126">An import collection helps guarantee that the seller is paid when the buyer collects the shipping documents to take delivery of the imported goods.</span></span>
