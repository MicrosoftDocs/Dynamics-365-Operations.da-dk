[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="50d24-104">Denne opgaveguide anvender demofirmaet USMF.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50d24-104">This task guide uses the USMF demo company.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="50d24-105">Det opretter et nyt anlægsaktiv ved hjælp af listesiden Anlægsaktiv.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50d24-105">It will create a new fixed asset using the Fixed asset list page.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="50d24-106">Gå til **Navigationsrude > Moduler > Anlægsaktiver > Anlægsaktiver > Anlægsaktiver**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50d24-106">Go to **Navigation pane > Modules > Fixed assets > Fixed assets > Fixed assets**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="50d24-107">Klik på **Ny** i **handlingsruden**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50d24-107">On the **Action pane**, click **New**.</span></span>

4. <span data-ttu-id="50d24-111">Skriv en værdi i feltet **Navn**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50d24-111">In the **Name** field, type a value.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="50d24-112">Angiv yderligere oplysninger, som din virksomhed skal bruge til dette anlægsaktiv.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50d24-112">Enter the additional information that your business needs for this asset.</span></span>  
5. <span data-ttu-id="50d24-113">Klik på **Bøger** i **handlingsruden**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50d24-113">On the **Action pane**, click **Books**.</span></span>
