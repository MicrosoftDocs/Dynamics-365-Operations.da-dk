# <a name="travel-requisitions"></a><span data-ttu-id="50c63-104">Rejserekvisitioner</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50c63-104">Travel requisitions</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="50c63-105">En *rejserekvisition* er et kildedokument, som viser de udgifter, der påløber i forbindelsen med rejsen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50c63-105">A *travel requisition* is a source document that lists the expenses that will be incurred for the purpose of travel.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="50c63-106">En rejserekvisition sendes til gennemsyn og kan derefter bruges til at godkende udgifter.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50c63-106">A travel requisition is submitted for review and can then be used to authorize expenses.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="50c63-107">Din organisation kan kræve, at en rejserekvisition indsendes, før medarbejdere får udgifter, som organisationen debiteres for.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50c63-107">Your organization might require that a travel requisition be submitted before employees incur any expense that is charged to the organization.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="50c63-108">Dette krav gælder, uanset om medarbejderne betaler udgifter vha. et firmakreditkort, bruger kontanter, som de har modtaget fra et kontant forskud, eller af egen lomme betaler udgifter, der så refunderes af organisationen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50c63-108">This requirement applies, regardless of whether employees charge expenses to a corporate credit card, spend cash that they received from a cash advance, or incur out-of-pocket expenses that will be reimbursed by the organization.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="50c63-109">Rejserekvisitioner og politikker kan bruges som hjælp til budgetstyring.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50c63-109">Travel requisitions and policies can be used to help with budget control.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="50c63-110">Hvis virksomheden f.eks. arbejder på et fastprisprojekt, der involverer rejseaktiviteter, skal rejseudgifterne for projektteamets medlemmer passe ind i budgettet for projektet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50c63-110">For example, if your organization is working on a fixed-price project that requires travel, the travel expenses of the project's team members must fit within the budget for the project.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="50c63-111">Ved at kræve, at rejseudgifter godkendes, før de afholdes, kan organisationen hjælpe med at sikre, at projektet holder sig inden for budgettet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50c63-111">By requiring that travel expenses be approved before they are incurred, the organization can help make sure that the project remains within budget.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="50c63-112">Du kan konfigurere politikker for rejserekvisitioner på siden **Rejserekvisition**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="50c63-112">You can set up policies for travel requisitions from the **Travel requisition** page.</span></span>
