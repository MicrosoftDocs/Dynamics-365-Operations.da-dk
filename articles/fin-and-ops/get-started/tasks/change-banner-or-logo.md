[!include [task guide banner](../../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="13336-104">Følgende procedure viser de trin, som systemadministratorer kan bruge til at opdatere banneret eller logo-billedet, der vises for en juridisk enhed.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="13336-104">The following procedure lists the steps that system administrators can use to update the banner or logo image that is displayed for a legal entity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="13336-105">Det demodatafirma, der bruges til at oprette denne procedure, er USMF.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="13336-105">The demo data company used to create this procedure is USMF.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="13336-106">Gå til Virksomhedsadministration > Organisationer > Juridiske enheder.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="13336-106">Go to Organization administration > Organizations > Legal entities.</span></span>

5. <span data-ttu-id="13336-112">Vælg Banner i feltet Dashboard, firmabilledtype.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="13336-112">In the Dashboard company image type field, select 'Banner'.</span></span>

    * <span data-ttu-id="13336-114">Tip!
