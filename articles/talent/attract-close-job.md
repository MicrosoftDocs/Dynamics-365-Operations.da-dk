## <a name="cancel-a-job"></a><span data-ttu-id="fd208-107">Annullere et job</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd208-107">Cancel a job</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="fd208-108">Du kan til enhver tid annullere et job i Attract.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd208-108">You can cancel a job in Attract at any time.</span></span>

   ![Lukke job i Attract](./media/attract-close-job.png)

3. <span data-ttu-id="fd208-112">Vælg **Job annulleret** i dialogboksen **Luk dette job**, tilføj eventuelle kommentarer, og vælg derefter **Luk job**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd208-112">In the **Close this job** dialog box, select **Job cancelled**, add any comments, and then select **Close job**.</span></span>

   ![Lukke job som Stilling besat](./media/attract-close-job-as-cancelled.png)

## <a name="close-a-job-as-position-filled"></a><span data-ttu-id="fd208-114">Lukke et job som Stilling besat</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd208-114">Close a job as Position filled</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="fd208-115">Du kan lukke et job som **Stilling besat**, når mindst én jobkandidat har fuldført tilbudsstadiet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd208-115">You can close a job as **Position filled** after at least one prospect has completed the Offer stage.</span></span>

2. <span data-ttu-id="fd208-117">Vælg **Luk job**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd208-117">Select **Close job**.</span></span>

   ![Lukke job i Attract](./media/attract-close-job.png)

3. <span data-ttu-id="fd208-119">Vælg **Stilling besat** i dialogboksen **Luk dette job**, vælg den kandidat, der har accepteret tilbuddet, tilføj eventuelle kommentarer, og vælg derefter **Luk job**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd208-119">In the **Close this job** dialog box, select **Position filled**, select the candidate who accepted the offer, add any comments, and then select **Close job**.</span></span>
