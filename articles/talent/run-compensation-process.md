# <a name="run-the-compensation-process"></a>Kør kompensationsprocessen

[!include [banner](includes/banner.md)]

**Afgang**

Kunden forsøger at køre kompensationsprocessen i Microsoft Dynamics 365 for Talent, uden at det lykkes.

**Årsag**

Kunden skal bruge dokumentationen.

**Løsning**

Her er dokumentationen til kompensation:

    - [Kompensationsordninger](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/dynamics365/unified-operations/talent/compensation-plans)
    - [Oprette faste kompensationsplaner](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/dynamics365/unified-operations/talent/create-fixed-compensation-plans)
    - [Oprette planer for variabel kompensation](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/dynamics365/unified-operations/talent/create-variable-compensation-plans)

- Proces:

    - [Proceskompensation](https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/dynamics365/unified-operations/talent/process-compensation)
