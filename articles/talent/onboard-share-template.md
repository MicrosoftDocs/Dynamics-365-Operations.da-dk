# <a name="share-content-with-other-contributors-in-dynamics-365-talent---onboard"></a><span data-ttu-id="e767f-103">Dele indhold med andre bidragydere i Dynamics 365 Talent - Onboard</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-103">Share content with other contributors in Dynamics 365 Talent - Onboard</span></span>

[!include [banner](includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="e767f-104">Microsoft Dynamics 365 Talent: Onboard giver dig mulighed for at dele onboardingguider og -skabeloner med andre bidragydere, så de kan gennemgå dokumenterne og foretage ændringer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-104">Microsoft Dynamics 365 Talent: Onboard lets you share onboarding guides and templates with other contributors so that they can review the documents and make changes.</span></span>

## <a name="share-an-onboarding-guide-with-other-contributors"></a><span data-ttu-id="e767f-105">Dele en onboardingguide med andre bidragydere</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-105">Share an onboarding guide with other contributors</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="e767f-106">Vælg **Guider** i menuen til venstre, og vælg derefter den guide, du vil dele.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-106">On the left menu, select **Guides**, and then select the guide that you want to share.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="e767f-107">Vælg knappen **Mere** (tre lodrette prikker) i øverste højre hjørne af siden, og vælg dernæst **Del**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-107">Select the **More** button (the three vertical dots) in the upper-right corner of the page, and then select **Share**.</span></span>

    ![[<span data-ttu-id="e767f-108">Deling af en onboardingguide med andre bidragydere</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-108">Sharing an onboarding guide with other contributors</span></span>](./media/onboard-share-guide.png)](./media/onboard-share-guide.png)

3. <span data-ttu-id="e767f-109">Under **Del med** kan du angive indstillingen **Kan disse personer foretage ændringer?** til **Til**, hvis modtagerne skal kunne revidere guiden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-109">Under **Share with**, if recipients should be able to revise the guide, set the **Can these people make edits?** option to **On**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e767f-110">Hvis modtagerne skal have besked via mail om, at du har delt guiden med dem, kan du angive indstillingen **Send mailbesked til modtagere** til **Til**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-110">If recipients should be notified by email that you've shared the guide with them, set the **Send email notification to recipients** option to **On**.</span></span>

    ![[<span data-ttu-id="e767f-111">Indstilling af adgangs- og mailindstillinger for en onboardingguide</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-111">Setting access and email preferences for an onboarding guide</span></span>](./media/onboard-share-guide-with-contributors.png)](./media/onboard-share-guide-with-contributors.png)

4. <span data-ttu-id="e767f-112">Indtast et navn eller en mailadresse, og vælg den ønskede modtager på rullelisten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-112">Enter a name or email address, and select the desired recipient in the drop-down list.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="e767f-113">Under fanen **Administrer adgang** kan du gennemse eller ændre, hvad modtagerne kan gøre med guiden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-113">On the **Manage access** tab, you can review or change what recipients can do with the guide.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e767f-114">Hvis du vil ændre en modtagers adgang, skal du på rullelisten ud for modtagerens navn vælge **Kan vise**, **Kan redigere** eller **Fjern**, når det er relevant.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-114">To change a recipient's access, in the drop-down list next to the recipient's name, select **Can View**, **Can edit**, or **Remove**, as appropriate.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="e767f-115">Når du er færdig med at tilføje eller ændre modtagere, skal du vælge **Opdater**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-115">When you've finished adding or modifying recipients, select **Update**.</span></span>

## <a name="share-an-onboarding-template-with-other-contributors"></a><span data-ttu-id="e767f-116">Dele en onboardingskabelon med andre bidragydere</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-116">Share an onboarding template with other contributors</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="e767f-117">Vælg **Skabeloner** i menuen til venstre.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-117">On the left menu, select **Templates**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="e767f-118">Under **Mine skabeloner** skal du vælge knappen **Mere** (ellipsen \[**...**\]) i øverste højre hjørne af den skabelon, du vil dele, og derefter vælge **Del**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-118">Under **My templates**, select the **More** button (the ellipsis \[**...**\]) in the upper-right corner of the template that you want to share, and then select **Share**.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="e767f-119">[![Deling af en skabelon med andre bidragydere](./media/onboard-share-template.png)](./media/onboard-share-template.png)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-119">[![Sharing a template with other contributors](./media/onboard-share-template.png)](./media/onboard-share-template.png)</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="e767f-120">Under **Modtager** kan du angive indstillingen **Kan disse personer foretage ændringer?** til **Til**, hvis modtagerne skal kunne revidere skabelonen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-120">Under **Recipient**, if recipients should be able to revise the template, set the **Can these people make edits?** option to **On**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e767f-121">Hvis modtagerne skal have besked via mail om, at du har delt skabelonen med dem, kan du angive indstillingen **Send mailbesked til modtagere** til **Til**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-121">If recipients should be notified by email that you've shared the template with them, set the **Send email notification to recipients** option to **On**.</span></span>

    [!Opsætning af adgangs- og mailindstillinger for en skabelon]<span data-ttu-id="e767f-122">(./media/onboard-share-template-access-and-email.png)](./media/onboard-share-template-access-and-email.png)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-122">(./media/onboard-share-template-access-and-email.png)](./media/onboard-share-template-access-and-email.png)</span></span>

4. <span data-ttu-id="e767f-123">Indtast et navn eller en mailadresse, og vælg den ønskede modtager på rullelisten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-123">Enter a name or email address, and select the desired recipient in the drop-down list.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="e767f-124">Under fanen **Administrer adgang** kan du gennemse eller ændre, hvad modtagerne kan gøre med skabelonen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-124">On the **Manage access** tab, you can review or change what recipients can do with the template.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e767f-125">Hvis du vil ændre en modtagers adgang, skal du på rullelisten ud for modtagerens navn vælge **Kan vise**, **Kan redigere** eller **Fjern**, når det er relevant.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-125">To change a recipient's access, in the drop-down list next to the recipient's name, select **Can View**, **Can edit**, or **Remove**, as appropriate.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="e767f-126">Når du er færdig med at tilføje eller ændre modtagere, skal du vælge **Opdater**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e767f-126">When you've finished adding or modifying recipients, select **Update**.</span></span>
