# <a name="leave-and-absence-management"></a><span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-103">Styring af orlov og fravær</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-103">Leave and absence management</span></span>

[!include [banner](includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-104">Modulet **Orlovs- og fraværsstyring** giver en fleksibel struktur til definition af fraværsstyringsprocessen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-104">The **Leave and absence management** module offers a flexible framework for defining the absence management process.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-105">Orlov og fraværsplaner kan oprettes for at finde ud af, hvordan medarbejdere periodiserer eller tildeles fridage.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-105">Leave and absence plans can be created to determine how employees accrue or are granted time off.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-106">Når medarbejderne er tilmeldt en plan, kan de sende anmodninger om fravær til godkendelse af ledere.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-106">After employees are enrolled in a plan, they can submit time-off requests for approval by managers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-107">Med orlovssporing kan både ledere på øverste niveau og personalechefer se, hvem der holder fri, og hvor lang tid hver enkelt medarbejder stadig holder fri.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-107">Leave tracking lets both first-level managers and Human Resources (HR) managers see who is taking time off and how much time off each employee still has.</span></span>  

<span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-108">Orlovs- og fraværsstyring indeholder følgende funktioner:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-108">Leave and absence management provides the following features:</span></span> 

- <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-109">**Definer orlovs- og fraværstyper.**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-109">**Define leave and absence types.**</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-110">Orlovstyper bruges til at definere de forskellige typer fravær, som medarbejdere kan rapportere.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-110">Leave types define the various types of absences that employees can report.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-111">Da disse typer er brugerdefinerede, kan de tilpasses til din organisation.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-111">Because these types are user-defined, they can be tailored to your organization.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-112">Nogle typiske orlovstyper omfatter betalt fravær (PTO), orlov, kortvarigt handicap, deltagelse i jury (denne orlovstype er specifikt for USA), og dødsfald.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-112">Some typical leave types include paid time off (PTO), leave, short-term disability, jury duty (this leave type is specific to the United States), and bereavement.</span></span> 

- <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-113">**Definer orlovs- og fraværsplaner, der er udviklet, så de passer til din virksomhed.**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-113">**Define leave and absence plans that are tiered to fit your business.**</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-114">Orlovs- og fraværsplaner kan defineres, så periodisering forekommer ved bestemte frekvenser, f.eks. en gang om året, månedligt eller hver 14. dag.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-114">Leave and absence plans can be defined so that accrual occurs at specific frequencies, such as annually, monthly, or semimonthly.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-115">Planer kan også defineres som en bonus, hvor en enkelt periodisering forekommer på en bestemt dato.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-115">Plans can also be defined as a grant, where a single accrual occurs on a specific date.</span></span> 

    <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-116">Orlovsplaner indeholder ofte niveauer, hvor visse grupper af medarbejdere får yderligere fordele baseret på det tidsrum, de har været i organisationen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-116">Leave plans often contain tiers, where some groups of employees receive additional benefits, based on the amount of time that they have been with the organization.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-117">Disse brugerdefinerede niveauer giver mulighed for automatisk kvalificering til frynsegodetimer, baseret på de datokriterier, der er defineret.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-117">These user-defined tiers enable automatic enrollment in additional benefit hours, based on the date criteria that are defined.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-118">Orlovsplaner kan også konfigureres til at gennemtvinge et maksimalt overførselsbeløb eller en minimumsaldo, for at forhindre, at medarbejdere bruger flere frynsegodetimer, end de har periodiseret.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-118">Leave plans can also be configured to enforce a maximum carry-over amount or a minimum balance, to prevent employees from using more benefit hours than they have accrued.</span></span> 

    <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-119">Typiske orlovsplaner indeholder niveaudelte planer, der giver et frynsegode ved 80 timers PTO til nye medarbejdere, men et frynsegode på 120 timer efter 60 måneders tjeneste.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-119">Typical leave plans include tiered plans that grant a benefit of 80 hours of PTO to new employees but a benefit of 120 hours after 60 months of service.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-120">Yderligere planer kan tildele variable feriedage eller stillingsbaserede frynsegoder, f.eks. frynsegodetimer for ledere.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-120">Additional plans might grant floating holidays or position-based benefits, such as executive-only benefit hours.</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-121">**Tilmeld medarbejdere til orlovs- og fraværsplaner enkeltvis eller via massetildeling, der er baseret på jobkriterier.**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-121">**Enroll employees in leave and absence plans individually or through mass assignment that is based on job criteria.**</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-122">Flere jobrelaterede filtre kan bruges til at tildele en gruppe af medarbejdere til en orlovsplan.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-122">Several job-related filters can be used to assign a group of employees to a leave plan.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-123">Personalechefer kan få vist en medarbejder for at se, hvilke orlovs- og fraværsplaner medarbejderen er tilmeldt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-123">HR managers can view an employee to see what leave and absence plans the employee is enrolled in.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-124">Personalechefer kan også se alle planer og de relaterede medarbejdertilmeldinger.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-124">Alternatively, HR managers can view all plans and the related employee enrollments.</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-125">**Afbryd orlovs- og fraværsplaner.**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-125">**Suspend leave and absence plans.**</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-126">Orlovs- fraværsplaner kan suspenderes for at deaktivere dem og forhindre yderligere periodiseringer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-126">Leave and absence plans can be suspended to make them inactive and prevent additional accruals.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-127">Periodiseringer suspenderes for medarbejdere, hvis deres ansættelse slutter.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-127">Accruals are suspended for employees if their employment ends.</span></span>  

- <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-128">**Juster rettigheder og bonusser.**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-128">**Adjust entitlements and grants.**</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-129">En medarbejder kan tilmeldes et højere niveau i planen ved hjælp af en arbejderspecifik dato for at tilsidesætte medarbejderens standardplanforslag.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-129">An employee can be enrolled in a higher plan tier by using a worker-specific date to override the employee's default plan offering.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-130">Medarbejderne kan modtage en manuel regulering af deres orlovs- og fraværssaldo på tidspunktet for tilmelding til planen, eller Personale kan foretage en manuel regulering til enhver tid.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-130">Employees can receive a manual adjustment to their leave and absence balance at the time of plan enrollment, or HR can make a manual adjustment at any time.</span></span> 

- <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-131">**Anvende en arbejdsgang på fraværsanmodninger.**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-131">**Apply a workflow to time-off requests.**</span></span>

     <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-132">En arbejdsgang kan tilpasses og anvendes på anmodninger om fritid for at opfylde organisationens behov.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-132">A workflow can be customized and applied to time-off requests to meet the organization’s requirements.</span></span>  

- <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-133">**Spor medarbejderfraværssaldi.**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-133">**Track employee absence balances.**</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-134">Medarbejdere, deres chefer og Personale kan få vist saldi for orlov og fravær.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-134">Employees, their manager, and HR can view leave and absence balances.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-135">Personale kan bruge interaktive rapporter til at spore tilmeldinger til planer og fritidssaldi efter type.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-135">HR can use interactive reports to track plan enrollments and time-off balances by type.</span></span> 

- <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-136">**Send anmodninger om fridage.**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-136">**Submit time-off requests.**</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-137">Medarbejdere kan sende anmodninger om fritid i forhold til deres ledige timer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-137">Employees can submit time-off requests against their available hours.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-138">Det kan være anmodninger om én enkelt dag eller om flere dage, der omfatter flere forskellige på orlovs- og fraværstyper.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-138">Requests can be simple single-day requests or multiple-day requests that include multiple leave and absence types.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-139">Hvis der ikke er aktiveret en arbejdsgang, godkendes anmodningerne automatisk.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-139">If a workflow isn't enabled, the requests are automatically approved.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6f4c8-140">Hvis en arbejdsgang er aktiveret, kan godkendelsen ske automatisk, eller den kan kræve godkendelse, afhængigt af arbejdsgangskonfigurationen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6f4c8-140">If a workflow is enabled, the approval can be automatic, or it can require sign-off, depending on the workflow configuration.</span></span>
