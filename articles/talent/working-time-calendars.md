[!include [banner](includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="3d31e-104">Med arbejdstidskalenderen kan du oprette en kalender med de timer og dage, hvor medarbejderne arbejder i din organisation.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-104">The working time calendar enables you to create a calendar with the hours and days that employees work in your organization.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-105">Kalendere strømliner som standard anmodningsprocessen for fravær i timer eller dage.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-105">Calendars streamline the time off request process by default in the hours or days.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-106">Når en medarbejder sender en anmodning om fravær, behøver de ikke at tænke på helligdage og lukninger, der er håndteret for dem via arbejdstidskalenderen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-106">When an employee submits a time off request, they don't have to worry about holidays and closures, which is handled for them through the working time calendar.</span></span>

## <a name="setting-up-a-working-time-calendar"></a><span data-ttu-id="3d31e-107">Konfigurer en arbejdstidskalender</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-107">Setting up a working time calendar</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3d31e-108">Kalendere omfatter genereringsoplysninger, de dage og timer, du vil have medtaget, dagene i kalenderen, arbejdstider for disse dage samt tilmeldte medarbejdere.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-108">Calendars include generation details, the days and hours you want to included, the days of the calendar, working times for those days as well as enrolled employees.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="3d31e-109">Følg disse trin for at konfigurere en kalender:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-109">To set up a calendar, follow these steps:</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-110">Klik på **Kalendere** på siden **Organisationsadministration**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-110">On the **Organization Administration** page, click **Calendars**.</span></span>

2. <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-111">I handlingsruden skal du vælge **Ny** og indtaste et navn og en beskrivelse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-111">On the Action Pane, select **New** and enter a name and description.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-112">Vælg arbejdsdage for organisationen, og angiv arbejdstid.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-112">Choose the work days for your organization and enter work time.</span></span>

4. <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-113">Tilføj helligdage og lukninger ved hjælp af knappen **Tilføj**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-113">Add Holidays and closures by using the **Add** button.</span></span>

5. <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-114">Angiv navnet på og beskrivelsen af helligdage og lukninger, f.eks. danske helligdage og andre lukkedage.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-114">Enter the name and description for the holidays and closures, such as US holidays or Bank holidays.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-115">Angiv datoerne for helligdage og lukninger.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-115">Enter the dates for the holidays and closures.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-116">Gem listen over helligdage og lukninger, og luk siden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-116">Save the Holidays and closures list and close the page.</span></span>

6. <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-117">Vælg listen over helligdage og lukninger i rullemenuen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-117">Select the holidays and closures list from the drop-down menu.</span></span>

7. <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-118">Hvis dine medarbejdere har ikke-arbejdstid, f.eks. frokostpauser eller andre pauser, kan du tilføje dem også.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-118">If your employees have non-work time, such as lunches or breaks, add those as well.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-119">Vælg **Ikke-arbejdstid**, og angiv navnet og tidsintervallet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-119">Select **Non-work time** and enter the name and time range.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-120">Luk siden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-120">Close the page.</span></span> 

8. <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-121">Klik på **Tilføj** for at tilføje ikke-arbejdstiden i din kalender.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-121">Click **Add** to add the non-work time to your calendar.</span></span>

9. <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-122">Under fanen **Dage** skal du vælge **Generér** for at generere dagene i kalenderen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-122">On the **Days** tab, select **Generate** to generate the days in your calendar.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-123">Angiv datointervallet for kalenderen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-123">Enter the date range for the calendar.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-124">Dagene og arbejdstiderne oprettes på basis af de arbejdsdage og -tider, der er defineret under genereringsindstillinger, sammen med de datoer, der er valgt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-124">The days and working times are generated based on the work days and times defined under the generation options in conjunction with the dates selected.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="3d31e-127">Medarbejdere er ikke forpligtet til at have kalendere tildelt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-127">Employees aren't required to have calendars assigned.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-128">Hvis en arbejdstidskalender er defineret, udelades fridage automatisk i anmodningen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-128">If there is a working time calendar defined, off days are automatically excluded from the request.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-129">Omfanget i timer eller dage bruger som standard de arbejdstider, der er defineret i kalenderen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-129">The amount, in hours or days, defaults to the working times defined in the calendar.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3d31e-130">Hvis medarbejderen ikke er tildelt en kalender, er alle dage tilgængelige for fravær, og længden af fraværet er ikke standard for anmodningen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3d31e-130">If an employee doesn't have a calendar assigned, all days are available for time off and the amount of time off is not the default for the request.</span></span>
