<span data-ttu-id="5c819-107">Common Data Service har ikke et tilsvarende koncept.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c819-107">Common Data Service doesn't have an equivalent concept.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5c819-108">Det tætteste koncept er *virksomhedsenhed*, som primært er en sikkerheds- og synlighedsgrænse for brugerdata.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c819-108">The closest concept is *business unit*, which is primarily a security and visibility boundary for user data.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5c819-109">Dette koncept har ikke samme juridiske eller forretningsmæssige implikationer som firmakonceptet i Finance and Operations.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c819-109">This concept doesn't have the same legal or business implications as the company concept in Finance and Operations.</span></span>

+ <span data-ttu-id="5c819-117">Der oprettes et separat ejerteam med samme navn.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c819-117">A separate owner team is created that has the same name.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5c819-118">Det er også tilknyttet virksomhedsenheden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c819-118">It's also associated with the business unit.</span></span>
