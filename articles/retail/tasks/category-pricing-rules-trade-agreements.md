11. <span data-ttu-id="2346c-118">Klik på Næste.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2346c-118">Click Next.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="2346c-119">Indtast en dato i feltet Fra dato.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2346c-119">In the From date field, enter a date.</span></span>

19. <span data-ttu-id="2346c-129">Indtast et tal i feltet Beløb i valuta.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2346c-129">In the Amount in currency field, enter a number.</span></span>
