# <a name="create-a-legal-entity"></a><span data-ttu-id="0c098-103">Oprette en juridisk enhed</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-103">Create a legal entity</span></span>

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="0c098-104">En juridisk enhed er en organisation, der er identificeret ved at være registreret hos en juridisk myndighed.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-104">A legal entity is an organization that is identified through registration with a legal authority.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-105">Juridiske enheder kan indgå kontrakter og skal udarbejde opgørelser med afrapportering af deres præstation.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-105">Legal entities can enter into contracts and are required to prepare statements that report on their performance.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-106">I følgende procedure beskrives det, hvordan en juridisk enhed oprettes.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-106">The following procedure explains how to create a legal entity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-107">Det demodatafirma, der bruges til at oprette denne procedure, er USMF.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-107">The demo data company used to create this procedure is USMF.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-108">Gå til **Navigationsrude > Moduler > Virksomhedsadministration > Organisationer > Juridiske enheder**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-108">Go to **Navigation pane > Modules > Organization administration > Organizations > Legal entities**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-109">Klik på **Ny**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-109">Click **New**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-110">Skriv en værdi i feltet **Navn**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-110">In the **Name** field, type a value.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-111">Skriv en værdi i feltet **Firma**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-111">In the **Company** field, type a value.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-112">Indtast eller vælg en værdi i feltet **Land/område**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-112">In the **Country/region** field, enter or select a value.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-113">Klik på **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-113">Click **OK**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-114">Sektionen **Generelt** indeholder følgende generelle oplysninger om den juridiske enhed: Angiv et søgenavn, hvis der kræves et navn til søgningen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-114">In the **General** section, provide the following general information about the legal entity: Enter a search name, if a search name is required.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-115">Et søgenavn er et alternativt navn, der kan bruges til at søge efter den pågældende juridiske enhed.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-115">A search name is an alternate name that can be used to search for this legal entity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-116">Angiv, om den pågældende juridiske enhed skal bruges til et konsolideret regnskab.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-116">Select whether this legal entity is being used as a consolidation company.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-117">Angiv, om den pågældende juridiske enhed skal bruges til et elimineringsregnskab.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-117">Select whether this legal entity is being used as an elimination company.</span></span> 
7. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-118">Udvid sektionen **Adresser**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-118">Expand the **Addresses** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-119">Klik på **Rediger** for at angive adresseoplysninger, som f.eks. gadenavn, husnummer, postnummer og by, i sektionen **Adresser**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-119">In the **Addresses** section, click **Edit** to enter address information, such as the street name and number, postal code, and city.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-120">Udvid sektionen **Kontaktoplysninger**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-120">Expand the **Contact information** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-121">Angiv oplysninger om kommunikationsmåder som f.eks. mailadresser, URL-adresser og telefonnumre, i sektionen **Kontaktoplysninger**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-121">In the **Contact information** section, enter information about methods of communication, such as email addresses, URLs, and telephone numbers.</span></span> 
9. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-122">Udvid sektionen **Lovpligtig rapportering**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-122">Expand the **Statutory reporting** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-123">Angiv de registreringsnumre, der bruges til lovpligtig rapportering, i sektionen **Lovpligtig rapportering**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-123">In the **Statutory reporting** section, enter the registration numbers that are used for statutory reporting.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-124">Udvid sektionen **Registreringsnumre**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-124">Expand the **Registration numbers** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-125">Angiv de oplysninger, der kræves af den juridiske enhed, i sektionen **Registreringsnumre**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-125">In the **Registration numbers** section, enter any information required by the legal entity.</span></span>  
11. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-126">Udvid sektionen **Bankkontooplysninger**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-126">Expand the **Bank account information** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-127">Angiv bankkonti og registreringsnumre for den juridiske enhed, i sektionen **Bankkontooplysninger**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-127">In the **Bank account information** section, enter bank accounts and routing numbers for the legal entity.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-128">Udvid sektionen **Udenrigshandel og logistik**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-128">Expand the **Foreign trade and logistics** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-129">Angiv forsendelsesoplysninger for den juridiske enhed, i sektionen **Udenrigshandel og logistik**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-129">In the **Foreign trade and logistics** section, enter shipping information for the legal entity.</span></span>  
13. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-130">Udvid sektionen **Nummerserier**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-130">Expand the **Number sequences** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-131">Du kan se de nummerserier, der er tilknyttet den juridiske enhed, i sektionen **Nummerserier**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-131">In the **Number sequences** section, you can view the number sequences that are associated with the legal entity.</span></span>  
14. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-132">Udvid afsnittet **Billeder**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-132">Expand the **Images** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-133">Vis eller skift det logo og/eller dashboardbillede, der er tilknyttet den juridiske enhed, i sektionen **Billeder**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-133">In the **Images** section, view or change the logo and/or dashboard image that are associated with the legal entity.</span></span>  
15. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-134">Udvid sektionen **Momsregistrering**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-134">Expand the **Tax registration** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-135">Angiv de registreringsnumre, der bruges til rapportering til skattemyndigheder, i sektionen **Momsregistrering**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-135">In the **Tax registration** section, enter the registration numbers that are used to report to tax authorities.</span></span>
16. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-136">Udvid sektionen **1099-skat**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-136">Expand the **Tax 1099** section.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="0c098-137">Angiv 1099-oplysninger for den juridiske enhed i sektionen **1099-skat**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-137">In the **Tax 1099** section, enter 1099 information for the legal entity.</span></span>  
17. <span data-ttu-id="0c098-138">Klik på **Gem**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0c098-138">Click **Save**.</span></span>
