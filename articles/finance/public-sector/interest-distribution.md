# <a name="set-up-interest-distribution-for-cash-accounts"></a><span data-ttu-id="915f5-104">Konfigurere rentefordeling for kontantkonti</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-104">Set up interest distribution for cash accounts</span></span>

[!include[banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="915f5-105">Din institution kan tildele (fordele) renten på en bankkonto til bestemte finanskonti baseret på den gennemsnitlige daglige saldo for kontantkonti.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-105">Your agency can allocate (distribute) the interest on a bank account to specific General ledger accounts, based on the average daily balance in cash accounts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915f5-106">Du kan bruge denne proces til at oprette en avanceret finanspost for rentebeløbene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-106">You can use this process to generate an advanced ledger entry for the interest amounts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915f5-107">Du kan også generere rentebeløbene til gennemsyn uden at bogføre dem.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-107">Alternatively, you can generate the interest amounts for review, without posting them.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="915f5-108">Før du fordeler renten, skal du oprette dine bankkonti på siden **Rentefordelingsregler**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-108">Before you distribute the interest, you must set up your participating cash accounts on the **Interest distribution rules** page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915f5-109">Hver kombination af en kontantkonto og en bonus kan bruges til at beregne renter for en anden rentekonto.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-109">Each combination of a cash account and a grant can be used to calculate interest for a different interest account.</span></span>

## <a name="add-a-cash-account-for-interest-distribution"></a><span data-ttu-id="915f5-110">Tilføje en kontantkonto til rentefordeling</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-110">Add a cash account for interest distribution</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="915f5-111">Gå til **Finans \> Opsætning \> Bogføring \> Rentefordelingsregler**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-111">Go to **General ledger \> Setup \> Posting \> Interest distribution rules**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="915f5-112">Vælg **Ny** for at tilføje en række.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-112">Select **New** to add a row.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="915f5-113">I feltet **Kontantkonto** skal du angive den kontantkonto, der indeholder en del af de samlede kontanter.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-113">In the **Cash account** field, enter the cash account that contains part of the total pooled cash.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="915f5-114">Valgfrit: I feltet **Bonus** skal du angive bonusbeløbet for bonusrelaterede posteringsbeløb, der skal medtages i beregningen af kontantsaldoen for den tilknyttede kontantkonto.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-114">Optional: In the **Grant** field, enter the grant for grant-related transaction amounts that should be included in cash balance calculations for the associated cash account.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915f5-115">Hvis alle bonusser for kontantkontoen bogfører renter på den samme konto, kan du lade dette felt være tomt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-115">If all grants for the cash account post interest to the same account, you can leave this field blank.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915f5-116">Men hvis du angiver en rentekonto for en bestemt bonus, skal du angive en rentekonto for hver bonus til kontantkontoen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-116">However, if you enter an interest account for a specific grant, you must enter an interest account for each grant for the cash account.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="915f5-117">Markér afkrydsningsfeltet **Deltag** for at medtage kontantkontiene i rentefordeling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-117">Select the **Participate** check box to include the cash accounts in interest distribution.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915f5-118">Hvis markeringen i afkrydsningsfeltet fjernes, kan du ikke redigere andre felter i rækken.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-118">If this check box is cleared, you can't edit any other fields in the row.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915f5-119">Kontantkonti, der ikke indgår, medtages på forespørgselssiden, så du kan gennemse den gennemsnitlige daglige saldo.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-119">Cash accounts that don't participate are included in the inquiry page so that you can review the average daily balance.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915f5-120">Du kan dog ikke fordele renter på disse kontantkonti.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-120">However, you can't distribute interest to those cash accounts.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="915f5-121">Angiv enten en bestemt rentekonto eller et projekt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-121">Enter either a specific interest account or a project.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915f5-122">Den værdi, du angiver, bestemmer, hvilken finanskonto renten bogføres på for den tilknyttede kontantkonto.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-122">The value that you enter determines the General ledger account that interest is posted to for the associated cash account.</span></span>

    - <span data-ttu-id="915f5-123">Angiv en specifik konto i feltet **Rentekonto**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-123">In the **Interest account** field, enter a specific account.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="915f5-124">I feltet **Projekt-id** skal du angive et projekt, der skal bruges som grundlag for bogføring af renter.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-124">In the **Project ID** field, enter a project to use as the basis for posting interest.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915f5-125">Denne konto bruges sammen med andre kontofaktorer til at bestemme hele den konto, der bogføres til.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-125">This account is used in conjunction with other account factors to determine the complete account that is posted to.</span></span>

7. <span data-ttu-id="915f5-126">Marker afkrydsningsfeltet **Negativ rente** for at beregne et negativt rentebeløb, når kontantkontoen har en negativ saldo.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-126">Select the **Negative interest** check box to calculate a negative interest amount when the cash account has a negative balance.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915f5-127">Hvis markeringen i dette afkrydsningsfelt fjernes, rapporterer kontantkontoen renten som 0 (nul) i stedet for et negativt beløb.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-127">If this check box is cleared, the cash account reports the interest as 0 (zero) instead of a negative amount.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="915f5-128">Rentekontoen for én kontantkonto kan modtage eventuelle øredifferencebeløb, når der beregnes rentefordeling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-128">The interest account for one cash account can receive any penny difference amounts when interest distribution is calculated.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915f5-129">Markér afkrydsningsfeltet **Afrunding** for den pågældende kontantkonto.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-129">Select the **Rounding** check box for that cash account.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915f5-130">Du kan kun markere én kontantkonto som afrundingskontoen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-130">You can mark only one cash account as the rounding account.</span></span>

## <a name="distributing-interest"></a><span data-ttu-id="915f5-131">Fordele renter</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-131">Distributing interest</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="915f5-132">Gå til **Finans \> Periodisk \> Fordeling \> Rentefordeling**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-132">Go to **General ledger \> Periodic \> Allocation \> Interest distribution**.</span></span>

    - <span data-ttu-id="915f5-135">I felterne **Fra dato** og **Til dato** skal du vælge det datointerval, der skal beregnes gennemsnitlige daglige saldi for, baseret på posteringer i datointervallet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-135">In the **From date** and **To date** fields, select the range of dates to calculate average daily balances for, based on transactions during that date range.</span></span>

    > [!NOTE]
    > <span data-ttu-id="915f5-143">Hvis du ændrer rentebeløbene, men derefter vil nulstille dem til de beregnede rentebeløb, skal du vælge **Parametre** og derefter vælge **Fordel** for at generere renten ved hjælp af de oprindelige data.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-143">If you change the interest amounts but then want to reset them to the calculated interest amounts, select **Parameters**, and then select **Distribute** to generate the interest by using the original data.</span></span>

    - <span data-ttu-id="915f5-145">Angiv regnskabsdatoen for den avancerede finanspost i feltet **Regnskabsdato**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-145">In the **Accounting date** field, enter the accounting date for the Advanced ledger entry.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="915f5-146">Vælg den projektkategori, der skal bruges til varerne i den avancerede finanspost, i feltet **Projektkategori**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-146">In the **Project category** field, select the project category to use for the items in the Advanced ledger entry.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915f5-147">Projektkategorien bestemmer også, hvilken hovedkonto den avancerede finanspost bogføres til.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-147">The project category also determines the main account that the Advanced ledger entry posts to.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="915f5-148">Vælg den bogføringsdefinition, der skal bruges til den avancerede finanspost, i feltet **Bogføringsdefinition**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-148">In the **Posting definition** field, select the posting definition to use for the Advanced ledger entry.</span></span>

6. <span data-ttu-id="915f5-149">Vælg **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-149">Select **OK**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915f5-150">En meddelelse viser nummeret på den avancerede finanspost, der automatisk oprettes.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915f5-150">A message shows the number of the Advanced ledger entry that is automatically created.</span></span>
