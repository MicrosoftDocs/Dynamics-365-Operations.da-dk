[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="6fb53-104">Dette emne viser, hvordan du konfigurerer standardskabeloner for økonomiske dimensioner.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6fb53-104">This topic shows how to set up financial dimension default templates.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6fb53-105">Skabelonerne kan bruges til hurtigt at distribuere beløb på kildedokumenterne.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6fb53-105">The templates can be used to quickly distribute amounts on source documents.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="6fb53-106">Gå i navigationsruden til **Moduler > Finans > Diagram over konti > Dimensioner > Standardskabeloner for økonomiske dimensioner**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6fb53-106">In the navigation pane, go to **Modules > General ledger > Chart of accounts > Dimensions > Financial dimension default templates**.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="6fb53-108">Angiv et skabelon-id i feltet **Skabelon-id**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6fb53-108">In the **Template ID** field, enter a Template ID.</span></span>

6. <span data-ttu-id="6fb53-111">Vælg **Tilføj**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6fb53-111">Select **Add**.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="6fb53-112">Angiv en anden procentdel for de næste dimensionsværdier.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6fb53-112">Enter another percent for the next dimension values.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6fb53-113">I dette eksempel skal du angive en procentdel, så du stadig har tildelt mindre end 100 %.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6fb53-113">For this example, enter a percentage so you still have less than 100% allocated.</span></span>

9. <span data-ttu-id="6fb53-115">Vælg **Tilføj**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6fb53-115">Select **Add**.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="6fb53-116">Angiv en anden procent, hvilket bevirker, at den samlede procentdel bliver lig med 100 %.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6fb53-116">Enter another percent, which will make the total percent equal to 100%.</span></span>

12. <span data-ttu-id="6fb53-118">Vælg **Gem**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6fb53-118">Select **Save**.</span></span>
