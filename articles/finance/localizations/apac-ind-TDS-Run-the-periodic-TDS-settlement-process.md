# <a name="run-the-periodic-tds-settlement-process"></a><span data-ttu-id="c44ed-103">Køre den periodiske skatteudligningsproces</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-103">Run the periodic TDS settlement process</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="c44ed-104">Dette emne forklarer, hvordan du udligner periodisk kildeskat (TDS – Tax Deducted at Source).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-104">This topic explains how to settle periodic Tax Deducted at Source (TDS).</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-105">Gå til **Skat \> Angivelser \> A-skat \> Betaling af A-skat**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-105">Go to **Tax \> Declarations \> Withholding tax \> Withholding tax payment**.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-106">[![Dialogboksen Betaling af A-skat](./media/apac-ind-TDS-47.png)](./media/apac-ind-TDS-47.png)</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-106">[![Withholding tax payment dialog box](./media/apac-ind-TDS-47.png)](./media/apac-ind-TDS-47.png)</span></span>

2. <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-107">Vælg **Kildeskat** i feltet **Skattetype** i dialogboksen **Betaling af A-skat**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-107">In the **Withholding tax payment** dialog box, in the **Tax type** field, select **TDS**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-108">Vælg det skattekontonummer (TAN), som udligningsprocessen skal køres for, i feltet **Skattekontonummer (TAN)**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-108">In the **Tax Account Number (TAN)** field, select the Tax Account Number (TAN) to run the settlement process for.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-109">Vælg den skattekomponentgruppe, der skal køres udligningsproces for, i feltet **Komponentgruppe for A-skat**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-109">In the **Withholding tax component group** field, select the TDS component group to run the settlement process for.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-110">I feltet **Udligningsperiode** skal du vælge den skatteudligningsperiode, som udligningsprocessen skal køres for.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-110">In the **Settlement period** field, select the TDS settlement period to run the settlement process for.</span></span>

    > [!NOTE]
    > <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-111">Skatteudligningsprocessen køres for alle perioder, der er konfigureret for skatteudligningsperioden under fanen **Perioder** på siden **Udligningsperioder for A-skat** (**Skat \> Indirekte skatter \> A-skat \> Udligningsperioder for A-skat**).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-111">The TDS settlement process is run for all periods that are set up for the TDS settlement period on the **Periods** tab of the **Withholding tax settlement periods** page (**Tax \> Indirect taxes \> Withholding tax \> Withholding tax settlement periods**).</span></span>

6. <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-112">I feltet **Fra dato** skal du vælge den startdato, som skatteudligningsprocessen skal køres fra.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-112">In the **From date** field, select the start date to run the TDS settlement process from.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-113">For en bestemt periode i udligningsperioden tages den startdato, der er defineret for perioden, som "Fra"-datoen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-113">For a specific period within the settlement period, the start date that is defined for the period is taken as the "from" date.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-114">Skatteudligningsperioden har f.eks. to perioder: 1. april til og med 30. april 2009 og 1. maj til og med 31. maj 2009.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-114">For example, the TDS settlement period has two periods: April 1 through April 30, 2009, and May 1 through May 31, 2009.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-115">Hvis du vælger **04/06/2009** (6. april 2009) som startdato i feltet **Fra dato**, køres udligningsprocessen stadig fra 1. april 2009.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-115">If you select **04/06/2009** (April 6, 2009) as the start date in the **From date** field, the settlement process will still run from April 1, 2009.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-116">Hvis du angiver en senere periode i feltet **Fra dato**, men uden at udligne en tidligere periode inden for udligningsperioden, vil udligningen ikke finde sted for nogen tidligere perioder.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-116">If you enter a later period in the **From date** field, but without settling a previous period within the settlement period, the settlement won't occur for any previous periods.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-117">Skatteudligningsperioden har f.eks. tre perioder: 1. april til og med 30. april 2009, 1. maj til og med 31. maj 2009 og 1. juni til og med 30. juni 2009.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-117">For example, the TDS settlement period has three periods: April 1 through April 30, 2009, May 1 through May 31, 2009, and June 1 through June 30, 2009.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-118">Hvis du vælger **05/01/2009** (1. maj 2009) som startdato i feltet **Fra dato**, køres udligningsprocessen kun fra 1. maj til og med 31. maj 2009.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-118">If you select **05/01/2009** (May 1, 2009) as the start date in the **From date** field, the settlement process will run only from May 1 through May 31, 2009.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-119">Udligningen foretages ikke for 1. april til og med 30. april 2009.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-119">The settlement won't occur for April 1 through April 30, 2009.</span></span>

7. <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-120">I feltet **Transaktionsdato** skal du vælge den dato, hvor du vil bogføre transaktionen for skatteudligningen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-120">In the **Transaction date** field, select the date to post the TDS settlement transaction.</span></span>
8. <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-121">Vælg skatteudligningsversionen i feltet **Betalingsversion for A-skat**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-121">In the **Withholding tax payment version** field, select the TDS settlement version:</span></span>

     - <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-122">**Original** – Brug denne indstilling til at køre skatteudligningsprocessen første gang.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-122">**Original** – Use this option to run the TDS settlement process for the first time.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-123">Den oprindelige betalingsversion bruges kun én gang til at køre skatteudligningsprocessen for en kombination af et skattekontonummer, en komponentgruppe for A-skat og en udligningsperiode for A-skat.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-123">The original payment version is used only one time to run the TDS settlement process for a combination of a TAN, a withholding tax component group, and a withholding tax settlement period.</span></span>
    - <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-124">**Seneste versioner** – Brug denne indstilling, hvis skatteudligningsprocessen allerede er kørt for den angivne periode.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-124">**Latest versions** – Use this option if the TDS settlement process has already been run for the specified period.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-125">Medtag tilbagedaterede transaktioner, der er bogført, efter den tidligere kørsel af udligningsprocessen for perioden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-125">Include back-dated transactions that were posted after the settlement process was previously run for the period.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-126">Du kan bruge denne indstilling til at køre udligningsprocessen flere gange.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-126">You can use this option to run the settlement process any number of times.</span></span>

9. <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-127">Markér afkrydsningsfeltet **Opdater** for at køre skatteudligningsprocessen og bogføre beløbene på finanskontiene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-127">Select the **Update** check box to run the TDS settlement process and post the amounts to the ledger accounts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-128">Hvis afkrydsningsfeltet ikke er markeret, køres udligningsprocessen ikke, og der genereres ikke finansposter.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-128">If this check box is cleared, the settlement process won't be run, and the financial entries won't be generated.</span></span>
10. <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-129">Vælg **OK** for at køre skatteudligningsprocessen og generere betalingsrapporten for A-skat.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-129">Select **OK** to run the TDS settlement process and generate the withholding tax payment report.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-130">Status for skattetransaktioner, der er inkluderet i udligningsprocessen, vises som **Udlignet** på siden **Udligning** (gå til **Kreditor \> Betalinger \> Kreditorbetalingskladde**, vælg **Linjer**, vælg **Funktioner**, og vælg derefter **Udligning**).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-130">The status of TDS transactions that are included in the settlement process is shown as **Settled** on the **Settlement** page (go to **Accounts payable \> Payments \> Vendor payment journal**, select **Lines**, select **Functions**, and then select **Settlement**).</span></span>

### <a name="important-points"></a><span data-ttu-id="c44ed-131">Vigtige punkter</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-131">Important points</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-132">Hvis en komponentgruppe for A-skat ikke er valgt under udligningsprocessen, foretages udligningen for alle komponentgrupper for A-skat for det skattekontonummer og den udligningsperiode, der er valgt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-132">If a withholding tax component group isn't selected during the settlement process, the settlement occurs for all the withholding tax component groups for the selected TAN and settlement period.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-133">Der oprettes en separat linje for hver komponentgruppe for A-skat på siden **Åbenpost-redigering**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-133">A separate line is created for each withholding tax component group on the **Open transaction editing** page.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-134">Udligningsprocessen baseres på arten af vurderingskategorien for en udligningsperiode.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-134">The settlement process is based on the nature of assessee category for a settlement period.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-135">De transaktioner, hvor arten af vurderingskategorien er **Virksomhed**, udlignes og vises som én linje på siden **Åbenpost-redigering**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-135">Transactions where the nature of assessee category is **Company** are settled and are shown as one line on the **Open transaction editing** page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-136">De transaktioner, hvor arten af vurderingskategorien er noget andet end **Virksomhed**, udlignes og vises som én linje på siden **Åbenpost-redigering**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-136">Transactions where the nature of assessee is something other than **Company** are settled and are shown as one line on the **Open transaction editing** page.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-137">Forfaldsdatoen for de udlignede skattetransaktionslinjer på siden **Åbenpost-redigering** er baseret på de betalingsbetingelser, der er defineret for skattemyndighedskreditoren på siden **Kreditorer**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-137">The due date for the settled TDS transaction lines on the **Open transaction editing** page is based on the terms of payment that are defined for the TDS authority vendor on the **Vendors** page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c44ed-138">Hvis betalingsbetingelserne ikke er defineret for skattemyndighedskreditoren, vises den sidste dag i udligningsperioden som forfaldsdatoen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c44ed-138">If the terms of payments aren't defined for the TDS authority vendor, the last day of the settlement period is shown as the due date.</span></span>
