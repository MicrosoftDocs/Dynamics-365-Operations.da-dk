# <a name="tax-directives-for-europe"></a><span data-ttu-id="6cc19-104">Momsvejledninger for Europa</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6cc19-104">Tax directives for Europe</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="6cc19-105">EU-direktivet indeholder bestemmelser, der skal følges af alle medlemsstater, med hensyn til faktureringsregler og oplysninger, der skal medtages, når der oprettes fakturaer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6cc19-105">EU Directive provides regulations to be followed by all Member States regarding invoice rules and information that must be included when creating invoices.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6cc19-106">EU-direktivet indeholder de oplysninger, der kræves af leverandører for deres kunder i EU.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6cc19-106">EU Directive lists the information that is required by suppliers for their customers in the EU.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6cc19-107">F.eks. kan en faktura indeholde en reference, der beskriver de juridiske grunde til en momssats på nul eller en momsundtagelse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6cc19-107">For example, an invoice could include a reference that describes the legal grounds for a VAT zero-rating or an exemption.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6cc19-108">Hvis en faktura justerer en tidligere udstedt faktura, f.eks. en kreditnota, skal der findes en utvetydig reference til den oprindelige faktura.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6cc19-108">If an invoice adjusts an earlier issued invoice, such as a credit note, an unambiguous reference should be made to the original invoice.</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="6cc19-109">Hvis du vil udskrive en beskrivelse af momskoder på en salgsfaktura, fritekstfaktura eller projektfakturarapport, skal du konfigurere momsvejledningens referencetekst på momskoderne.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6cc19-109">To print a description of sales tax codes on a sales invoice, free text invoice, or project invoice report, set up the Tax Directive reference text on the sales tax codes.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="6cc19-110">For kreditnotaer udskrives nummeret og datoen for den oprindelige faktura sammen med årsagen til rettelsen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6cc19-110">For credit notes, the number and date of the original invoice are printed along with the reason for correction.</span></span>

> [!NOTE]
> <span data-ttu-id="6cc19-111">På siden **Formularopsætning** (**Debitor** > **Opsætning** > **Formularer** > **Formularopsætning** på fanen **Generelt**) skal du kontrollere, at feltet **Momsspecifikation** er angivet til **Registreringsvaluta** eller **Registrerings- og firmavaluta**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6cc19-111">Verify on the **Form setup** page (**Accounts receivable** > **Setup** > **Forms** > **Form setup**, on the **General tab**), that the **Sales tax specification** field is set to **Registration currency** or **Registration and company currency**.</span></span>
