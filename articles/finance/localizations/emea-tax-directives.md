# <a name="tax-directives-for-europe"></a><span data-ttu-id="e7f87-104">Momsvejledninger for Europa</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7f87-104">Tax directives for Europe</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="e7f87-105">EU-direktivet indeholder bestemmelser, der skal følges af alle medlemsstater, med hensyn til faktureringsregler og oplysninger, der skal medtages, når der oprettes fakturaer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7f87-105">EU Directive provides regulations to be followed by all Member States regarding invoice rules and information that must be included when creating invoices.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e7f87-106">EU-direktivet indeholder de oplysninger, der kræves af leverandører for deres kunder i EU.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7f87-106">EU Directive lists the information that is required by suppliers for their customers in the EU.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e7f87-107">F.eks. kan en faktura indeholde en reference, der beskriver de juridiske grunde til en momssats på nul eller en momsundtagelse.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7f87-107">For example, an invoice could include a reference that describes the legal grounds for a VAT zero-rating or an exemption.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e7f87-108">Hvis en faktura justerer en tidligere udstedt faktura, f.eks. en kreditnota, skal der findes en utvetydig reference til den oprindelige faktura.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7f87-108">If an invoice adjusts an earlier issued invoice, such as a credit note, an unambiguous reference should be made to the original invoice.</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="e7f87-109">Hvis du vil udskrive en beskrivelse af momskoder på en salgsfaktura, fritekstfaktura eller projektfakturarapport, skal du konfigurere momsvejledningens referencetekst på momskoderne.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7f87-109">To print a description of sales tax codes on a sales invoice, free text invoice, or project invoice report, set up the Tax Directive reference text on the sales tax codes.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="e7f87-110">For kreditnotaer udskrives nummeret og datoen for den oprindelige faktura sammen med årsagen til rettelsen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7f87-110">For credit notes, the number and date of the original invoice are printed along with the reason for correction.</span></span>

> [!NOTE]
> <span data-ttu-id="e7f87-111">Kontrollér, at feltet **Momsspecifikation** er angivet til **Registreringsvaluta** eller **Registrerings- og firmavaluta** på siden **Formularopsætning** på fanen **Generelt** (**Debitor > Konfiguration > Formularer > Formularopsætning** eller **Projektstyring og regnskab > Konfiguration > Formularer > Formularopsætning**).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7f87-111">Verify that the **Sales tax specification** field is set to **Registration currency** or **Registration and company currency** on the **Form setup** page on the **General** tab (**Accounts receivable > Setup > Forms > Form setup** or **Project management and accounting > Setup > Forms > Form setup**).</span></span>
