# <a name="establish-customer-payment-fees"></a><span data-ttu-id="45632-103">Fastlægge gebyrer for debitorbetaling</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-103">Establish customer payment fees</span></span>

[!include [banner](../../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="45632-104">Opret betalingsgebyrer for debitorbetalinger.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-104">Create payment fees for customer payments.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="45632-105">Denne opgave bruger demofirmaet USMF.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-105">This task uses the USMF demo company.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="45632-106">I **navigationsruden** skal du gå til **Moduler > Debitor > Betalingsopsætning > Betalingsgebyr**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-106">In the **Navigation pane**, go to **Modules > Accounts receivable > Payments setup > Payment fee**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="45632-107">Klik på **Ny**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-107">Click **New**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="45632-108">Angiv et gebyr-id i feltet **Gebyr-id**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-108">In the **Fee ID** field, enter a Fee ID.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-109">Gebyr-id'et vises i betalingskladder, så gør det så beskrivende, at det fremgår, hvilket gebyr der vurderes.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-109">The Fee ID displays on payment journals, so make it descriptive to understand what fee is being assessed.</span></span>  
4. <span data-ttu-id="45632-110">Skriv et gebyrnavn i feltet **Navn**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-110">In the **Name** field, enter a fee name.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="45632-111">Indtast en beskrivelse af gebyret i feltet **Gebyrbeskrivelse**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-111">In the **Fee description** field, enter a description for the fee.</span></span>
6. <span data-ttu-id="45632-112">Vælg, om gebyret opkræves af kunden eller en finanskonto, i feltet **Gebyr**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-112">In the **Charge** field, select whether the fee will be charged to the Customer or a Ledger account.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-113">Hvis kunden har vurderer gebyret, skal du vælge Kunde.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-113">If the customer is assessed the fee, select Customer.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-114">Hvis gebyret skal henføres til din organisation som en udgift, skal du vælge Finans.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-114">If the fee will be assess to your organization as an expense, select Ledger.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-115">For denne opgave skal du vælge Debitor.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-115">For this task, select Customer.</span></span>  
7. <span data-ttu-id="45632-116">Vælg den kladdetype, der kan bruge dette betalingsgebyr, i feltet **Kladdetype**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-116">In the **Journal type** field, select the type of journal that can use this payment fee.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-117">Hvis disse gebyrer bruges til debitorbetalinger, vil kladdetypen sandsynligvis være Debitorbetaling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-117">If these fees are used for customer payments, the journal type will likely be Customer payment.</span></span>  
8. <span data-ttu-id="45632-118">Klik på **Gem**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-118">Click **Save**.</span></span>
9. <span data-ttu-id="45632-119">Klik på **Opsætning af betalingsgebyr**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-119">Click **Payment fee setup**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-120">Opsætningen Betalingsgebyr bruges til at definere kriterier for, hvornår betalingsgebyret vil blive vurderet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-120">The Payment fee setup is used to define the criteria for when the payment fee will be assessed.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="45632-121">Du kan for eksempel definere, at gebyret beregnes, hvis bankkontoen er USMF OPER, og betalingsmetoden er check.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-121">For example, you can define that the fee will be calculated if the bank account is USMF OPER, and the method of payment is check.</span></span>  
10. <span data-ttu-id="45632-122">Vælg enten Tabel, Gruppe eller Alle i feltet **Grupperinger** for at definere, hvilke bankkonti der skal pålignes dette gebyr.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-122">In the **Groupings** field, select either Table, Group or All to define which bank accounts will be assessed this fee.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-123">Hvis du vælger Alle, kan alle bankkonti blive pålignet dette gebyr.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-123">If you select All, all bank accounts could be assessed this fee.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="45632-124">Hvis du vælger Tabel, kan kun den bankkonto, som du vælger, blive pålignet dette gebyr.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-124">If you select Table, only the bank account you select could be assessed this fee.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-125">Hvis du vælger Gruppe, kan kun bankkontiene i den valgte bankgruppe blive pålignet dette gebyr.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-125">If you select Group, only the bank accounts in the selected bank group could be assessed this fee.</span></span>  
11. <span data-ttu-id="45632-126">Vælg enten en bankgruppe eller en bankkonto i feltet **Bankrelation**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-126">In the **Bank relation** field, select either a bank group or a bank account.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-127">Hvis du valgte Tabel, vil opslaget vise bankkonti.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-127">If you selected Table, the lookup will display bank accounts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-128">Hvis du valgte Gruppe, vil opslaget vise bankgrupper.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-128">If you selected Group, the lookup will display bank groups.</span></span>  
12. <span data-ttu-id="45632-129">Klik op linket i den valgte række på listen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-129">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>
13. <span data-ttu-id="45632-130">Vælg den betalingsmåde, som dette gebyr skal vurderes for, i feltet **Betalingsmåde**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-130">In the **Method of payment** field, select the Method of payment for which this fee will be assessed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-131">Du kan for eksempel påligne kunderne et gebyr, hvis de betaler via check i stedet for at bruge elektronisk betaling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-131">For example, you may assess a fee to your customers if they send payments as a check, rather than as an electronic payment.</span></span>  
14. <span data-ttu-id="45632-132">Find og vælg den ønskede post på listen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-132">In the list, find and select the desired record.</span></span>
15. <span data-ttu-id="45632-133">Angiv en betalingsvaluta i feltet **Betalingsvaluta**, hvis det er relevant.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-133">If relevant, in the **Payment currency** field, enter a payment currency.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-134">Betalingsvalutaen bruges som et yderligere kriterium for, om gebyret vil blive vurderet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-134">The payment currency is used as an additional criteria for whether the fee will be assessed.</span></span>  <span data-ttu-id="45632-135">Banken kan for eksempel opkræve et ekstra gebyr for betalinger i USD, da de normalt kun foretager transaktioner i EUR.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-135">For example, your bank may charge an extra fee for payments received in USD currency, since they normally only transact in EUR currency.</span></span>  
16. <span data-ttu-id="45632-136">Vælg, om gebyret skal være en procent, et beløb eller et interval.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-136">Select whether the fee will be a percent, amount or interval.</span></span>
17. <span data-ttu-id="45632-137">Angiv enten gebyrets procent eller beløb i feltet **Procent/beløb**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-137">In the **Percentage/Amount field**, enter either percentage or amount of the fee.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-138">Hvis feltet Procent/Beløb er Procent, angives værdien her som en procent.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-138">If the Percentage/Amount field is Percent, then the value enter here will be a percentage.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-139">Hvis feltet Procent/Beløb er Beløb, vil den værdi, du angiver her, være et beløb.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-139">If the Percentage/Amount field is Amount, then the value you enter here will be an amount.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-140">Hvis feltet Procent/Beløb er Interval, kan du bruge fanen Interval til at definere niveauerne.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-140">If the Percentage/Amount field is Interval, use the Interval tab to define the tiers.</span></span>  
18. <span data-ttu-id="45632-141">Vælg valutaen for gebyret i feltet **Valuta for gebyr**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-141">In the **Fee currency** field, select the currency of the fee.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45632-142">Dette er den valuta, som gebyret vil blive oprettet i.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-142">This is the currency in which the fee will be created.</span></span>  
19. <span data-ttu-id="45632-143">Klik på **Gem**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45632-143">Click **Save**.</span></span>
