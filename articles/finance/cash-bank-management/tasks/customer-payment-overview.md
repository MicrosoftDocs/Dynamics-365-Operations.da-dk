# <a name="customer-payment-overview"></a><span data-ttu-id="31af4-103">Oversigt over debitorbetalinger</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-103">Customer payment overview</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-106">Gå til **Navigationsrude > Moduler > Debitor > Betalinger > Betalingskladde**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-106">Go to **Navigation pane > Modules > Accounts receivable > Payments > Payment journal**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-107">Klik på **Ny**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-107">Click **New**.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-108">Vælg den betalingskladde, som debitorbetalingerne gemmes i.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-108">Select the payment journal which the customer payments will be saved.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-109">Vælg eller angiv kladden manuelt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-109">Select or manually enter the journal.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-110">Klik på **Angiv debitorbetalinger** i **handlingsruden**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-110">On **Action pane**, click **Enter customer payments**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-111">Angivelse af debitorbetalinger bruges til at registrere én debitorbetaling ad gangen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-111">Enter customer payments is used to record one customer payment at a time.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-112">Du angiver betalingsoplysningerne øverst, og derefter kan du markere de fakturaer, der blev betalt med betalingen fra den samme side.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-112">You enter the payment information at the top, and then you can mark the invoices that were paid by the payment, all from the same page.</span></span>  
6. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-113">Angiv den debitor, du modtog betalingen fra.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-113">Enter the customer from whom you received the payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-114">Hvis du ikke kender debitoren, men ved, at en transaktion er blevet betalt, kan du bruge feltet Transaktion til at angive betalingen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-114">If you don't know the customer but do know a transaction that was paid, you can use the Transaction field to enter the payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-115">Angiv eller vælg fakturaen i feltet Transaktion.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-115">Enter or select the invoice in the Transaction field.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-116">Debitoren bliver automatisk valgt som standard, når du har valgt transaktionen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-116">The customer will automatically default after you select the transaction.</span></span>
7. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-117">I feltet **Betalingsreference** skal du angive en betalingsreference.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-117">In the **Payment reference** field, enter a payment reference.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-118">Betalingsreferencen kan være debitorens checknummer eller en reference fra kundens elektronisk betaling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-118">The payment reference could be the customer's check number or a reference from the customer's electronic payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-119">Betalingsreferencen er kun påkrævet, hvis du markerer, at betalingen skal medtages på et indbetalingsbilag.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-119">The payment reference is only required if you mark to include the payment on a deposit slip.</span></span>  
8. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-120">Vælg, om betalingen skal medtages i et indbetalingsbilag i feltet **Indbetalingsbilag**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-120">In the **Deposit slip** field, select whether the payment will be included on a deposit slip.</span></span> 
9. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-121">Angiv debitorbetalingsbeløbet i feltet **Beløb**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-121">In the **Amount** field, enter the amount of the customer payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-122">Det indbetalte beløb angives ikke som et standardbeløb.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-122">The payment amount will not default.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-123">Det skal angives manuelt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-123">It must be manually entered.</span></span> 
10. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-124">Markér de fakturaer, der er betalt af debitoren.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-124">Mark the invoices that were paid by the customer.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-125">Hvis betalingen er en forudbetaling, behøver du ikke at markere en faktura til udligning.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-125">If the payment is a prepayment, you are not required to mark any invoices for settlement.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-126">Betalingen kan stadig gemmes og bogføres.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-126">The payment can still be saved and posted.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-127">Udligning til en faktura kan foregå på et senere tidspunkt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-127">Settlement to an invoice can happen at a later time.</span></span>
11. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-128">Angiv det samlede beløb på den betaling, der skal udlignes til den markerede faktura</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-128">Enter the amount of the payment that should be settled to the marked invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-129">Dette felt kan bruges, når betalingen er en delvis betaling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-129">This field can be used when the payment is for a partial payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-130">Hvis du ikke indtaster et beløb, fastlægges beløbet, der skal udlignes, automatisk for dig.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-130">If you don't enter an amount, the amount to settle will automatically be determined for you.</span></span>
12. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-131">Klik på **Gem i kladde**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-131">Click **Save in journal**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-132">Før du kan gemme betalingen til kladden, skal du sørge for, at modkontoen er defineret.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-132">Before you save the payment to the journal, make sure that the offset account is defined.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-133">Med **Gem i kladde** gemmes betalingen, og den flyttes til kladden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-133">Using **Save in journal** will save the payment and move it to the journal.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-134">Du kan derefter fortsætte med at angive den næste betaling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-134">You can then continue entering the next payment.</span></span>
13. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-135">Luk siden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-135">Close the page.</span></span>
14. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-136">Klik på Linjer i **handlingsruden**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-136">On **Action pane**, click Lines.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-137">Når du åbner Linjer, kan du se de betalinger, du har registreret på siden på **Angiv debitorbetalinger**, og som er gemt i kladden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-137">When opening Lines, you will see any payments you recorded on the **Enter customer payments** page and saved into the journal.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-138">Du kan også bruge denne side til at angive nye debitorbetalinger eller redigere eksisterende debitorbetalinger, før de bogføres.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-138">You can also use this page to enter new customer payments, or edit existing customer payment before they are posted.</span></span>
15. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-139">Klik på **Ny** for at oprette en anden betaling.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-139">Click **New** to create another payment.</span></span> 
16. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-140">Vælg den debitor, du modtog betalingen fra.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-140">Select the customer from whom you received the payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-141">Hvis du ikke kender debitoren, men ved, at en faktura er betalt via betalingen, kan du bruge feltet Faktura til manuelt at indtaste eller vælge fakturaen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-141">If you don't know the customer but know an invoice paid by the payment, use the Invoice field to manually enter or select the invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-142">Debitoren bliver angivet som standard, når fakturaen er valgt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-142">The customer will default after the invoice is selected.</span></span>  
17. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-143">Klik på **Udlign transaktioner** for at markere fakturaer, der er betalt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-143">Click **Settle transactions** to mark invoices that were paid.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-144">Du behøver ikke at udligne betalinger til fakturaer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-144">You are not required to settle the payment to any invoices.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-145">Hvis dette er en forudbetaling, eller hvis du ikke ved, hvilken fakturaen der er betalt, kan du indtaste og bogføre betalingen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-145">If this is a prepayment or if you don't know what invoice was paid, you can enter and post the payment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-146">Betalingen kan udlignes med en faktura på et senere tidspunkt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-146">The payment can be settled to an invoice at a later point.</span></span>  
18. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-147">Markér fakturaer, der er betalt via betalingen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-147">Mark the invoices paid by the payment.</span></span> 
19. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-148">Angiv beløbet på den betaling, der skal udlignes til fakturaen, i feltet **Beløb**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-148">In the **Amount** field, enter the amount of the payment that will be settled to the invoice.</span></span>
20. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-149">Klik på **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-149">Click **OK**.</span></span>
21. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-150">I feltet **Betalingsreference** skal du angive en betalingsreference.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-150">In the **Payment reference** field, enter a payment reference.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="31af4-151">Betalingsreferencen er kun påkrævet, hvis du markerer, at betalingen skal medtages på et indbetalingsbilag.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-151">The payment reference is only required if you mark to include the payment on a deposit slip.</span></span>  
22. <span data-ttu-id="31af4-152">Klik på **Bogfør** i **handlingsruden** for at bogføre debitorbetalinger.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="31af4-152">On **Action pane**, click **Post** to post the customer payments.</span></span>
