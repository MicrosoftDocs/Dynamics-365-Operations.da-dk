[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-104">Remburser er bankdokumenter, der ofte bruges ved køb og salg af varer på tværs af internationale grænser.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-104">Letters of credit are bank documents that are commonly used for the purchase and sale of goods across international borders.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-105">Remburser bruges i internationale transaktioner til at sikre betalinger.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-105">Letters of credit are used for international transactions to ensure that payments will be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-106">En remburs er en aftale, der udfærdiges af en bank, hvor banken accepterer at sikre betaling på vegne af køberen, hvis vilkårene i aftalen mellem køberen og sælgeren overholdes.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-106">A letter of credit is an agreement that is issued by a bank, in which the bank agrees to ensure payment on behalf of the buyer, if the terms of the agreement between the buyer and seller are met.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-107">Bemærk, at en remburs også kaldes et akkreditiv.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-107">Note that a letter of credit is also referred to as a documentary credit (DC).</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-108">I en importremburs er den juridiske enhed køberen eller ansøgeren til rembursen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-108">For an import letter of credit, the legal entity is the buyer or the applicant for the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-109">I en eksportremburs er den juridiske enhed sælgeren eller beneficianten af rembursen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-109">For an export letter of credit, the legal entity is the seller or the beneficiary of the letter of credit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-110">Følgende parter er involveret i en kreditnota:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-110">The following parties are involved with a letter of credit:</span></span> 

 - <span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-111">Rekvirenten (køber), som har til hensigt at betale for varerne</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-111">The applicant (buyer) who intends to pay for the goods</span></span> 
 - <span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-112">Beneficianten (sælger), som skal modtage betalingen</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-112">The beneficiary (seller) who will receive the payment</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-113">Den udstedende bank, der udsteder rembursen</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-113">The issuing bank that issues the letter of credit</span></span>
 - <span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-114">Den adviserende bank, der udfører transaktionen på vegne af rekvirenten</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-114">The advising bank that carries out the transaction on behalf of the applicant</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-115">Rembursen indeholder en beskrivelse af varerne, evt. nødvendige dokumenter, afsendelsesdatoen og udløbsdatoen, efter hvilken betaling ikke vil blive foretaget.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-115">The letter of credit includes a description of the goods, any required documents, the date of shipment, and the expiration date after which payment will not be made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-116">Den udstedende bank opkræver et depositum for rembursen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-116">The issuing bank collects a margin for the letter of credit.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-117">En remburs kan enten være genkaldelig eller uigenkaldelig.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-117">A letter of credit can be revocable or irrevocable.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-118">Typen af en remburs kan være overdragelig, uoverdragelig eller revolverende.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-118">The nature of a letter of credit can be transferable, non transferable, or revolving.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="4e4f8-119">En remburs er typisk en uigenkaldelig og bekræftet aftale om, at betaling vil blive foretaget til en bestemt beneficiant ved fremsendelse af fuldstændig og korrekt forsendelsesdokumentation.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="4e4f8-119">Typically, a letter of credit is an irrevocable and confirmed agreement that payment will be made to a specific beneficiary upon submission of complete and accurate shipping documentation.</span></span>
