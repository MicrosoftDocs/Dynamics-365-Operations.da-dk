# <a name="adjust-leases"></a><span data-ttu-id="9589c-104">Justere leasingaftaler</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-104">Adjust leases</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="9589c-105">Emnet forklarer, hvordan du justerer en leasingaftale.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-105">The topic explains how to adjust a lease.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9589c-106">Det kan være nødvendigt at foretage en justering, hvis betingelserne for leasingaftalen ændres, leasingaftalen forlænges, eller andre betingelser ændres.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-106">Adjustment might be required if the lease terms are modified, the lease is extended, or other circumstances change.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9589c-107">Aktivleasing er i overensstemmelse med retningslinjerne for, at Accounting Standards Codification Topic 842 (ASC 842) og International Financial Reporting Standard 16 (IFRS 16) omhandler redigering af leasingaftaler.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-107">Asset leasing complies with the guidance that Accounting Standards Codification Topic 842 (ASC 842) and International Financial Reporting Standard 16 (IFRS 16) provide about lease modifications.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9589c-108">ASC-842-20-15-1 definerer en leasingændring, som enhver ændring af vilkårene og betingelserne i en kontrakt, der medfører en ændring i omfanget af eller overvejelser i forbindelse med en leasingaftale.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-108">ASC 842-20-15-1 defines a lease modification as any change to the terms and conditions of a contract that causes a change in the scope of, or the consideration for, a lease.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9589c-109">Punkt 39 i IFRS 16 angiver, at en af modtagerne skal regulere leasingforpligtelsen, så den afspejler ændringer af betalinger af leasingaftalen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-109">Paragraph 39 of IFRS 16 states that a lessee must revalue the lease liability so that it reflects changes to the lease payments.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="9589c-110">For organisationer, der overholder ASC 842 eller IFRS 16, revurderes en leasingaftale, så den afspejler en ændring i den nuværende værdi af de fremtidige minimums leasingbetalinger (PVFMLP).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-110">For organizations that adhere to ASC 842 or IFRS 16, a lease is remeasured to reflect a change in the present value of the future minimum lease payments (PVFMLP).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9589c-111">Hvis PVFMLP øges, vil den oprettede kladdepost være en debitering af kontoen for brugsretsaktivet (ROU) og en kreditering af leasingforpligtelsen for forskellen mellem den nye PVFMLP og den tidligere PVFMLP.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-111">If the PVFMLP increases, the journal entry that is created will be a debit to the right-of-use (ROU) asset and a credit to the lease liability for the difference between the new PVFMLP and the previous PVFMLP.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9589c-112">Hvis PVFMLP reduceres, vil kladdeposteringen være et debetbeløb af leasingforpligtelsen og en kreditering til ROU-aktivet for forskellen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-112">If the PVFMLP decreases, the journal entry will be a debit to the lease liability and a credit to the ROU asset for the difference.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="9589c-113">Hvis reguleringen reducerer ROU-aktivet til under 0 (nul), krediteres resten med den bogføringskonto for leasingændringer, der er angivet på siden **Leasingsaftalens bogføringskonti**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-113">If the adjustment decreases the ROU asset past 0 (zero), the remainder will be credited to the gain on lease modification posting account that is specified on the **Lease posting accounts** page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9589c-114">Systemkontiene for disse transaktioner og andre reguleringsposter, f. eks. klassificeringsændringer, indledende direkte omkostninger, leasingincitamenter, forudbetalinger og faste omkostninger, der opstår i forbindelse med leasingændringer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-114">The system accounts for these transactions and other adjustment entries, such as classification changes, initial direct costs, lease incentives, prepayments, and dismantling costs that arise from lease modifications.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="9589c-116">Følg disse trin for at justere en leasingaftale.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-116">To adjust a lease, follow these steps.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9589c-117">De ændrede data opdaterer leasingplanerne, når processen til oprettelse af en planlægningsproces er kørt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-117">The modified data will update lease schedules after the Create schedule process is run.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="9589c-121">Bemærk, at siden **Justér leasingaftale** ligner siden **Tilføj leasingsaftale**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-121">Notice that the **Adjust lease** page resembles the **Add lease** page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9589c-122">Ligesom den startdato, du angiver, når du tilføjer en leasingaftale, bestemmes, hvornår den nye leasingaftale begynder, bestemmer feltet **Ændret den** på siden **Justér leasingaftale**, hvornår den justerede leasingaftale starter.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-122">Additionally, just as the commencement date that you specify when you add a lease determines when the new lease starts, the **Modification date** field on the **Adjust lease** page determines when the modified lease starts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9589c-123">Denne dato kan ikke ligge efter startdatoen på betalingsplanlinjerne.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-123">This date can't be after the start date on the payment schedule lines.</span></span>

    > [!NOTE]
    > <span data-ttu-id="9589c-124">Felterne **ROU-overvejelser** gælder for reguleringen af leasingaftalen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-124">The **ROU considerations** fields apply to the lease adjustment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9589c-125">Hvis ingen af de indledende direkte omkostninger, leasingincitamenter, forudbetalinger eller faste omkostninger knyttes til den ændrede leasingaftale, skal du lade disse felter være tomme.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-125">If no initial direct costs, lease incentives, prepayments, or dismantling costs are associated with the modified lease, you should leave these fields blank.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9589c-126">De oprindelige beløb gælder ikke for den opdaterede leasingaftale.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-126">The original amounts won't apply to the updated lease.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9589c-127">Eventuelle yderligere omkostninger, der er påløbet, når leasingaftalen ændres, skal angives på siden **Justér leasingaftale**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9589c-127">Any additional costs that are incurred when the lease is modified should be entered on the **Adjust lease** page.</span></span>
    >
