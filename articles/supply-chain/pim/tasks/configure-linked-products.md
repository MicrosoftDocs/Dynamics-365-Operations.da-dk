<span data-ttu-id="35b24-104">Denne procedure gennemgår konfiguration af tilknyttede produkter.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35b24-104">This procedure walks through configuring linked products.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="35b24-105">Denne procedure bruger USRT-demodataene, og det antages, at begge produkter allerede er oprettet og frigivet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35b24-105">This procedure uses the USRT demo data, and assumes both products have already been created and released.</span></span>

9. <span data-ttu-id="35b24-114">Klik op linket i den valgte række på listen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="35b24-114">In the list, click the link in the selected row.</span></span>
