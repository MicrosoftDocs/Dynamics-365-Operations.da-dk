<span data-ttu-id="23f01-104">Serviceaftalegrupper er nyttige ved sortering og filtrering af serviceaftaler.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="23f01-104">Service agreement groups are useful for sorting and filtering service agreements.</span></span>
