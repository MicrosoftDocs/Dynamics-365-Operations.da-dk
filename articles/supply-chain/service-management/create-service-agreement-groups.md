<span data-ttu-id="8901a-104">Serviceaftalegrupper er nyttige ved sortering og filtrering af serviceaftaler.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8901a-104">Service agreement groups are useful for sorting and filtering service agreements.</span></span>
