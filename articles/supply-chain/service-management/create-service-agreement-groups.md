[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="257e3-104">Serviceaftalegrupper er nyttige ved sortering og filtrering af serviceaftaler.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="257e3-104">Service agreement groups are useful for sorting and filtering service agreements.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="257e3-105">Klik på **Servicestyring** \> **Opsætning** \> **Serviceaftaler** \> **Serviceaftalegrupper**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="257e3-105">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreement groups**.</span></span>
