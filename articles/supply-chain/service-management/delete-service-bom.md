# <a name="delete-a-service-bom"></a><span data-ttu-id="6dd71-103">Slette en servicestykliste</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6dd71-103">Delete a service BOM</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="6dd71-104">Klik på **Servicestyring** \> **Almindelige** \> **Serviceaftaler** \> **Serviceaftaler**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6dd71-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreements**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6dd71-105">Vælg den serviceaftale, du vil slette en styklistetjeneste fra.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6dd71-105">Select the service agreement that you want to delete a service bill of materials (BOM) from.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="6dd71-106">Klik på **Serviceobjekter** i **handlingsruden**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6dd71-106">On the **Action Pane**, click **Service objects**.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="6dd71-107">I formularen **Serviceobjekter** skal du vælge det serviceobjekt, som du vil slette styklistetjenesten fra.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6dd71-107">In the **Service objects** form, select the service object that you want to delete a service BOM from.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6dd71-108">Klik på **Funktioner**, og vælg derefter **Slet servicestykliste**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6dd71-108">Click **Functions**, and then select **Delete service BOM**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="6dd71-109">Klik på **Ja**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6dd71-109">Click **Yes**.</span></span>

> <P><span data-ttu-id="6dd71-110">Hvis du ikke har anvendt en styklisteskabelon, kan styklistetjenesten slettes fra formularen <STRONG>Styklisteskabeloner</STRONG>.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6dd71-110">If you have not applied a template BOM, the service BOM can be deleted from the <STRONG>Template BOMs</STRONG> form.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6dd71-111">Hvis du har anvendt styklisteskabelonen, kan styklistetjenesten ikke slettes på denne måde.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6dd71-111">If you have applied a template BOM, the service BOM cannot be deleted by using this method.</span></span></P>
