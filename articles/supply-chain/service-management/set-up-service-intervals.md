<span data-ttu-id="e2b37-104">Serviceintervallet angiver den frekvens, hvormed serviceordrelinjer oprettes til serviceaftalelinjer, når du opretter serviceordrer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e2b37-104">Service interval indicates the frequency with which service order lines are created for service agreement lines when you create service orders.</span></span>
