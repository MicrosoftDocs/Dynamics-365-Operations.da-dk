# <a name="attach-templates-to-service-objects"></a><span data-ttu-id="b8de7-103">Tilknytte skabeloner til serviceobjekter</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b8de7-103">Attach templates to service objects</span></span>    

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="b8de7-104">En styklisteskabelon kan knyttes til en serviceobjektrelation.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b8de7-104">A template bill of materials (BOM) can be attached to a service object relation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b8de7-105">Serviceobjektrelationen kan derefter tilknyttes serviceordrer eller serviceaftaler.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b8de7-105">The service object relation can then be associated with either service orders or service agreements.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="b8de7-106">Klik på **Servicestyring** \> **Opsætning** \> **Serviceobjekter** \> **Serviceobjekter**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b8de7-106">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service objects** \> **Service objects**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="b8de7-107">Vælg et serviceobjekt.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b8de7-107">Select a service object.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="b8de7-108">Klik på **Serviceaftaler** eller **Serviceordrer** for at få vist serviceaftaler eller serviceordrer, som serviceobjektet er tilknyttet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b8de7-108">Click **Service agreements** or **Service orders** to display the service agreements or service orders that the service object has been associated with.</span></span>

> [!TIP]
> <P><span data-ttu-id="b8de7-111">Hvis du vil flytte en tilknyttet stykliste til et andet serviceobjekt eller slette en tilknyttet stykliste, skal du i formularen <STRONG>Serviceobjekter</STRONG> klikke på <STRONG>Funktioner</STRONG> og derefter klikke på <STRONG>Flyt servicestykliste</STRONG> eller <STRONG>Slet servicestykliste</STRONG>.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b8de7-111">To move an attached BOM to another service object or to delete an attached BOM, in the <STRONG>Service objects</STRONG> form, click <STRONG>Functions</STRONG>, and then click <STRONG>Move service BOM</STRONG> or <STRONG>Delete service BOM</STRONG>.</span></span></P>
