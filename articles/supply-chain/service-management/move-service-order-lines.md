# <a name="move-service-order-lines"></a><span data-ttu-id="f0860-103">Flyt serviceordrelinjer</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f0860-103">Move service order lines</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="f0860-104">Klik på **Servicestyring** \> **Almindelige** \> **Serviceordrer** \> **Serviceordrer**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f0860-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service orders** \> **Service orders**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="f0860-105">Dobbeltklik på den serviceordre, der indeholder de linjer, du vil flytte til en anden serviceordre.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f0860-105">Double-click the service order that contains the lines that you want to move to another service order.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="f0860-106">I formularen **Serviceordrer** i handlingsruden skal du klikke på knappen **Flyt linjer**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f0860-106">In the **Service orders** form, on the Action Pane, click the **Move lines** button.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="f0860-107">I formularen **Flyt serviceordrelinjer** i feltet **Serviceordre** skal du vælge den serviceordre, du vil flytte linjerne til.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f0860-107">In the **Move service order lines** form, in the **Service order** field, select the service order that you want to move the lines to.</span></span>

6.  <span data-ttu-id="f0860-109">Klik på **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f0860-109">Click **OK**.</span></span>


> [!NOTE]
> <P><span data-ttu-id="f0860-110">Du kan kun flytte serviceordrelinjer mellem serviceordrer, der er tilknyttet den samme serviceaftale.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f0860-110">You can move service order lines only between service orders that are attached to the same service agreement.</span></span></P>
