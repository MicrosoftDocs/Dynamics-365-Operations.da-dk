# <a name="reassign-activities"></a><span data-ttu-id="c5629-103">Gentildele aktiviteter</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c5629-103">Reassign activities</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="c5629-104">Dette emne beskriver, hvordan du flytter tildelingen af serviceaktiviteter fra én arbejder til en anden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c5629-104">This topic describes how to reassign service activities from one worker to another worker.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c5629-105">Du kan nemt flytte tildelingen af serviceaktiviteter fra en arbejder til en anden, selvom disse arbejdere er tildelt til forskellige planlægningsteams.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c5629-105">You can reassign service activities from one worker to another worker even if those workers are assigned to different dispatch teams.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="c5629-106">Brug følgende trin til at tildele en aktivitet til en anden arbejder:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c5629-106">Use the following steps to reassign an activity to another worker:</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="c5629-107">Klik på **Servicestyring** \> **Periodisk** \> **Planlægningstavle**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c5629-107">Click **Service management** \> **Periodic** \> **Dispatch board**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="c5629-108">Hvis du vil definere en tidsperiode, som du vil have vist aktiviteter for, skal du angive datoer i felterne **Fra dato** og **Til dato**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c5629-108">In the **From date** and **To date** fields, enter dates to define the time period to view activities for.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="c5629-109">Vælg, om du vil have vist lukkede aktiviteter og forsendelsesoplysninger for relaterede teams, og klik derefter på **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c5629-109">Select whether to view closed activities and dispatch information for related teams, and then click **OK**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="c5629-110">Klik i formularen **Planlægningstavle** på **Avanceret** for kun at få vist Gantt-diagrammet øverst på siden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c5629-110">In the **Dispatch board** form, click **Advanced** to display only the Gantt chart at the top of the page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c5629-111">Klik på **Simpel** for at få vist Gantt-diagrammet og fanerne i formularen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c5629-111">Click **Simple** to display the Gantt chart and the tabs in the form.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="c5629-112">Udvid hver liste over planlægningsteams.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c5629-112">Expand each dispatch team list.</span></span>

6.  <span data-ttu-id="c5629-113">Brug et af følgende trin til at gentildele en aktivitet:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c5629-113">Use either of the following steps to reassign an activity:</span></span>
    
      - <span data-ttu-id="c5629-114">Marker en farvekodet grafik, der repræsenterer den serviceaktivitet, der skal gentildeles.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c5629-114">Select a color-coded graphic that represents the service activity to reassign.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c5629-115">Tryk på Skift-tasten, og flyt derefter den farvekodede grafik til rækken for en anden medarbejder.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c5629-115">Press the SHIFT key, and then move the color-coded graphic to the row for another employee.</span></span>
    
      - <span data-ttu-id="c5629-116">I feltet **Arbejder** under fanen **Alle**, fanen **Team**, fanen **Ikke planlagt** eller fanen **Relateret** skal du angive navnet på den arbejder, der erstatter den aktuelle arbejder for aktiviteten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c5629-116">On the **All** tab, **Team** tab, **Undispatched** tab, or **Related** tab, in the **Worker** field, enter the name of the worker who is replacing the current worker for the activity.</span></span>
