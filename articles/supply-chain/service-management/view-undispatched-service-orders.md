[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="d64ce-104">Dette emne beskriver, hvordan du får vist aktiviteter i en serviceordre, der ikke har en arbejder tildelt fra et planlægningsteam.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d64ce-104">This topic describes how to view activities in a service order that don’t have a worker assigned to them from a dispatch team.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="d64ce-105">Klik på **Servicestyring** \> **Periodisk** \> **Planlægningstavle**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d64ce-105">Click **Service management** \> **Periodic** \> **Dispatch board**.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="d64ce-107">Valgfrit: Vælg, om du vil medtage lukkede aktiviteter og relaterede teams i resultaterne.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d64ce-107">Optional: Select whether to include closed activities and related teams in the results.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="d64ce-108">Klik på **OK** for at se resultaterne.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d64ce-108">Click **OK** to view the results.</span></span>
    

    > [!NOTE]
    > <P><span data-ttu-id="d64ce-109">I formularen <STRONG>Planlægningstavle</STRONG> åbnes visningen <STRONG>Avanceret</STRONG> automatisk.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d64ce-109">In the <STRONG>Dispatch board</STRONG> form, the <STRONG>Advanced</STRONG> view is automatically displayed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d64ce-110">I denne visning vises Gantt-diagrammet øverst på siden og under fanerne nederst på siden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d64ce-110">In this view, the Gantt chart at the top of the page and the tabs at the bottom of the page are displayed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d64ce-111">Klik på <STRONG>Simpel</STRONG> for kun at få vist Gantt-diagrammet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d64ce-111">Click <STRONG>Simple</STRONG> to display only the Gantt chart.</span></span></P>
