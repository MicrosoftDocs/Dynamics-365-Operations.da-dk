# <a name="invoice-subscription-transactions"></a><span data-ttu-id="caafc-103">Fakturere abonnementstransaktioner</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="caafc-103">Invoice subscription transactions</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]



1.  <span data-ttu-id="caafc-104">Klik på **Servicestyring** \> **Almindelige** \> **Serviceabonnementer** \> **Alle serviceabonnementer**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="caafc-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **All service subscriptions**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="caafc-105">Vælg et serviceabonnement, og klik derefter på **Rediger**, eller klik på **Abonnement** for at oprette et serviceabonnement.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="caafc-105">Select a service subscription and then click **Edit** or click **Subscription** to create a service subscription.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="caafc-106">Klik på **Analyser** \> **Gebyrtransaktioner**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="caafc-106">Click **Analyze** \> **Fee transactions**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="caafc-107">Vælg den eller de gebyrtransaktioner, der skal faktureres, og klik derefter på **Funktioner** \> **Fakturaforslag** for at åbne formularen **Fakturaforslag**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="caafc-107">Select the fee transaction or fee transactions to invoice, and then click **Functions** \> **Invoice proposal** to open the **Invoice proposals** form.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="caafc-108">Angiv de relevante parametre til fakturaen i formularen **Opret faktura**, og klik derefter på **Opret faktura**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="caafc-108">Click **Create invoice** and enter the appropriate parameters in the **Create invoice** form</span></span>

6.  <span data-ttu-id="caafc-109">Klik på **OK** for at oprette fakturaforslag i formularen **Fakturaforslag**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="caafc-109">Click **OK** to create the invoice proposal in the **Invoice proposals** form.</span></span>

7.  <span data-ttu-id="caafc-110">Kontrollér indholdet på fakturaen, og klik på **Bogfør faktura** for at åbne formularen **Bogfør faktura** for at bogføre fakturaen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="caafc-110">Verify the contents of the invoice, click **Post invoice** to open the **Post invoice** form to post the invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="caafc-111">Du kan også udskrive fakturaen fra formularen **Bogfør faktura**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="caafc-111">You can also print the invoice from the **Post invoice** form.</span></span>
