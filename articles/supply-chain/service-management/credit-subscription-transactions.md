[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


## <a name="credit-subscription-transactions"></a><span data-ttu-id="aba3c-104">Kreditere abonnementstransaktioner</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="aba3c-104">Credit subscription transactions</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="aba3c-105">Klik på **Servicestyring** \> **Almindelige** \> **Serviceabonnementer** \> **Alle serviceabonnementer**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="aba3c-105">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **All service subscriptions**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="aba3c-106">Vælg det abonnement, der er tilknyttet abonnementstransaktionen, du vil oprette en kreditnota for.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="aba3c-106">Select the subscription attached to the subscription transaction for which you want to create a credit note.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="aba3c-107">Vælg fanen **Analyser**, og klik derefter på knappen **Gebyrtransaktioner** i handlingsruden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="aba3c-107">Select the **Analyze** tab, and then click the **Fee transactions** button on the Action Pane.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="aba3c-108">Vælg den transaktion, som du vil oprette en kreditnota til, i formularen **Gebyrtransaktioner**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="aba3c-108">From the **Fee transactions** form, select the transaction for which you want to create a credit note.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="aba3c-109">Klik på **Funktioner** \> **Vælg til kreditnota**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="aba3c-109">Click **Functions** \> **Select for credit note**.</span></span>

6.  <span data-ttu-id="aba3c-110">I formularen **Vælg til kreditnota** skal du vælge den transaktion, du vil kreditere, og derefter klikke på **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="aba3c-110">From the **Select for credit note** form, select the transaction that you want to credit and then click **OK**.</span></span>


> [!NOTE]
> <P><span data-ttu-id="aba3c-111">Når du opretter kreditnotaen, er det vigtigt, at du vælger <STRONG>Kreditnotaer</STRONG>.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="aba3c-111">When you create the credit note, make sure that you select <STRONG>Credit notes</STRONG>.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="aba3c-112">Du finder denne mulighed på listen <STRONG>Fakturametode</STRONG> i dialogboksen <STRONG>Opret faktura</STRONG>.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="aba3c-112">This is found in the <STRONG>Invoicing method</STRONG> list in the <STRONG>Create invoice</STRONG> dialog box.</span></span></P>

<span data-ttu-id="aba3c-113">Hvis feltet **Tilbagefør periodiseringer ved kreditering** i formularen **Parametre for servicestyring** er indstillet til **Manuelt**, skal du tilbageføre hver periodisering individuelt, før du opretter et kreditnotaforslag til transaktionen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="aba3c-113">If the **Reverse accruals on crediting** field in the **Service management parameters** form is set to **Manual**, you have to reverse each accrued revenue transaction individually before you create a credit note proposal for the transaction.</span></span>
