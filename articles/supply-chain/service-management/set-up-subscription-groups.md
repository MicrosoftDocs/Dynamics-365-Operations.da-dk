[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="afb6c-104">Klik på **Servicestyring** \> **Opsætning** \> **Serviceabonnementer** \> **Abonnementsgrupper**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="afb6c-104">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **Subscription groups**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="afb6c-105">Tryk på Ctrl+N, eller klik på **Ny** for at oprette en ny abonnementsgruppe.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="afb6c-105">Press Ctrl+N or click **New** to create a new subscription group.</span></span>
