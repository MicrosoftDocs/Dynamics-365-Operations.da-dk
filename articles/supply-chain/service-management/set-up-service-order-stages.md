[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="b7c0c-104">Klik på **Servicestyring** \> **Opsætning** \> **Serviceordrer** \> **Servicestadier**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b7c0c-104">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service orders** \> **Service stages**.</span></span>
