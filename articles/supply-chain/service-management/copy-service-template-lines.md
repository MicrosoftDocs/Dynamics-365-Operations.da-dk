2. <span data-ttu-id="bb148-107">Dobbeltklik på en serviceaftale for at åbne siden **Serviceaftaler**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb148-107">Double-click a service agreement to open the **Service agreements** page.</span></span>

7. <span data-ttu-id="bb148-112">Luk de forskellige sider for at gemme ændringerne.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bb148-112">Close the pages to save your changes.</span></span>
