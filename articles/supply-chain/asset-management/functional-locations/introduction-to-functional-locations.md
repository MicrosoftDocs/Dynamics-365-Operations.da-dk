<span data-ttu-id="fcb78-104">Dette emne indeholder en oversigt over arbejdssteder i Styring af aktiver.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fcb78-104">This topic provides an overview of functional locations in Asset Management.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="fcb78-105">Arbejdssteder er elementer af en teknisk struktur, såsom de funktionelle enheder i et system.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fcb78-105">Functional locations are elements of a technical structure, such as the functional units in a system.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="fcb78-106">Arbejdssteder oprettes hierarkisk, og du installerer aktiver på dem.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fcb78-106">Functional locations are created hierarchically, and you install assets on them.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="fcb78-107">Opsætningen af arbejdssteder i din virksomhed afhænger af virksomhedens behov.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fcb78-107">The setup of functional locations in your company depends on the company's requirements.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="fcb78-108">Her er nogle eksempler på, hvordan du kan bruge arbejdssteder:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fcb78-108">Here are some examples of how you can use functional locations:</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="fcb78-121">Arbejdssteder giver mulighed for at spore aktiver i relation til anmodninger, arbejdsordrer, fejlregistreringer, tilstandsvurderinger, registreringer af produktionsstop og registreringer af aktivtæller.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fcb78-121">Functional locations provide traceability of assets in relation to requests, work orders, fault registrations, condition assessments, production stop registrations, and asset counter registrations.</span></span>
