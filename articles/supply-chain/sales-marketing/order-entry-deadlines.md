<span data-ttu-id="d6f04-107">I mange firmaer er det kun salgsordrer, der modtages før et bestemt tidspunkt på dagen, der behandles som modtaget den pågældende dag.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d6f04-107">In many companies, only sales orders that are received before a certain time of day are treated as if they were received on that day.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d6f04-108">Alle ordrer, der modtages efter dette tidspunkt, behandles, som om de modtages den næste arbejdsdag.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d6f04-108">Any orders that are received after that time are treated as if they are received on the next business day.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d6f04-109">Dette skæringstidspunkt for ordrer kaldes ordreindtastningsfristen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d6f04-109">This cut-off time for orders is known as the order entry deadline.</span></span>
