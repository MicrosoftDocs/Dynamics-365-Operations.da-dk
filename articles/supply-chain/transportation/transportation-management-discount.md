# <a name="transportation-management-discounts"></a><span data-ttu-id="ea405-103">Rabatter i transportstyring</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ea405-103">Transportation management discounts</span></span>

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="ea405-104">Med transportstyring kan du konfigurere rabatter, der skal anvendes oven på den transportpris, der er beregnet af vurderingsprogrammet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ea405-104">Transportation management lets you set up discounts to be applied on top of the transportation price calculated by the rating engine.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ea405-105">Du kan angive hver rabat enten som en procentdel af gebyrer eller som et fast beløb.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ea405-105">You can specify each discount either as a percentage of charges or as a fixed amount.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ea405-106">Hvis du vil konfigurere rabatter for transportstyring, skal du gå til **Transportstyring \> Konfiguration \> Vurdering \> Rabatter**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ea405-106">To set up discounts for transportation management, go to **Transportation management \> Setup \> Rating \> Discounts**.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="ea405-107">Du kan vælge, om du vil overføre rabatten til kunden (ved at angive rabattypen for kunden) eller beholde den i virksomheden (ved at angive rabattypen til speditionsfirma).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ea405-107">You can choose whether to pass the discount on to your customer (by setting the discount type to customer) or to retain it in the company (by setting the discount type to shipper).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="ea405-108">Dette giver dig mulighed for at definere en opsætning, hvor du tjener en avance på de beregnede fragtomkostninger.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ea405-108">This lets you define a setup in which you earn a margin on the calculated freight charges.</span></span>
