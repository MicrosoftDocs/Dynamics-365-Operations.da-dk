### <a name="manual"></a><span data-ttu-id="5b706-126">Manuelt</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5b706-126">Manual</span></span>

### <a name="start"></a><span data-ttu-id="5b706-131">Start</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5b706-131">Start</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="5b706-132">Startrydningsprincippet angiver, at materialer forbruges automatisk, når produktionsordren startes.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5b706-132">The Start flushing principle indicates that material will be automatically consumed when the production order is started.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5b706-133">Mængden af materiale, der forbruges, er proportional med det antal, der startes.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5b706-133">The amount of material that is consumed is proportional to the quantity that is started.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5b706-134">Når startrydningsprincippet bruges sammen med produktionsudførelsessystemet, kan det også bruges til at rydde materialer, når en handling eller et procesjob startes.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5b706-134">When the Start flushing principle is used together with the manufacturing execution system, it can also be used to flush materials when an operation or a process job is started.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5b706-135">Dette princip er relevant, hvis f.eks. afvigelsen i forbruget er lav, materialerne er af lav værdi, der ikke er nogen krav til sporing, eller der er en kort kørselstid på handlinger.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5b706-135">This principle is relevant if, for example, the variance in the consumption is low, the materials are low-value materials, there are no tracking requirements, or there is a short run time on operations.</span></span>
