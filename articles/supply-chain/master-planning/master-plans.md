# <a name="master-plans"></a><span data-ttu-id="e7516-103">Behovsplaner</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-103">Master plans</span></span>

[!include[banner](../includes/banner.md)]


<span data-ttu-id="e7516-104">Brug forskellige behovsplaner, der understøtter firmaets daglige arbejdsrutiner, simulere forskellige planlægningsstrategier, som du vil overvåge, og implementer en firmapolitik, der f.eks. vedrører intern performance eller kundetilfredshed.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-104">Use various master plans to support your company's daily working operations, simulate different planning strategies that you want to monitor, and implement a company policy, such as a policy about internal performance or customer satisfaction.</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="e7516-105">Du kan konfigurere behovsplaner på siden **Behovsplaner**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-105">You can configure master plans on the **Master plans** page.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="e7516-106">Der findes to former for planer:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-106">There are two types of plans:</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="e7516-107">**Statisk plan** – Beregningen af varedisponering bruger de aktuelle data til at oprette en plan for nettobehov.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-107">**Static plan** – The master planning calculation uses the current data to generate a net requirements plan.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e7516-108">Denne plan forbliver uændret, indtil næste gang du kører varedisponering.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-108">This plan remains unchanged until the next time that you run master planning.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e7516-109">Det er en driftsplan, som forskellige medarbejdere i firmaet, f.eks. en indkøber eller produktionsplanlægger, kan bruge til at basere deres beslutninger på og udføre deres daglige opgaver og aktiviteter.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-109">It is an operating plan that various company personnel, such as a purchaser or production planner, can use to base their decisions on and perform their daily tasks and activities.</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="e7516-110">**Dynamisk plan** – Denne plan starter med den samme plan for nettobehov, der er genereret af varedisponeringen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-110">**Dynamic plan** – This plan starts with the same net requirements plan that was generated by master planning.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e7516-111">Du kan dog opdatere den dynamiske plan, hver gang der ændres i masterdata.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-111">However, you can update the dynamic plan every time that the master data changes.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e7516-112">Dette skyldes muligvis, at du f.eks. opretter en ny salgsordre.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-112">This could be when you create a new sales order, for example.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e7516-113">Det giver dig mulighed for at overvåge netværket med ordreændringer og varetilgængelighed uden at forstyrre den statiske plan, som andre bruger i deres arbejdsprocesser.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-113">This enables you to monitor the changing order network and item availability without disturbing the static plan that others are using for their work processes.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="e7516-114">Et firma kan vælge kun at arbejde med en dynamisk plan eller at bruge både en statisk og en dynamisk plan.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-114">A company may choose to work with just a dynamic plan or it may use both static and dynamic plans.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e7516-115">Derudover kan du konfigurere en behovsplan, der skal afspejle en bestemt strategi eller løse et problem.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-115">In addition, you can configure any master plan to reflect a particular strategy or address an issue.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e7516-116">Der er følgende eksempler:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-116">Examples are as follows:</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="e7516-117">Angiv højere lagerniveauer for at sikre sig mod manko.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-117">Set higher inventory levels to guarantee against stockouts.</span></span>
-   <span data-ttu-id="e7516-118">Angiv længere sikkerhedsmargener for at beskytte sig mod upålidelige leverandører.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-118">Set longer safety margins to protect against unreliable vendors.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="e7516-119">Du kan også angive, at den dynamiske startplan skal opdateres med den nye behovsplan, hver gang du kører varedisponering.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-119">You can also set up the starting dynamic plan so that it is updated with the new requirements plan every time that you run master planning.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e7516-120">Du kan angive disse indstillinger på siden **Varedisponeringsparametre**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e7516-120">You can specify these settings on the **Master planning parameters** page.</span></span>
