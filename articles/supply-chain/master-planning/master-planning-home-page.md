<span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-104">Kernefunktionen i Varedisponering gør det muligt for virksomheder at bestemme og afbalancere deres fremtidige behov for råmaterialer og evne til at opfylde virksomhedens mål.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-104">At its core, Master planning allows companies to determine and balance the future need for raw materials and capacity to meet company goals.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-105">Varedisponering vurderer følgende:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-105">Master planning assesses the following:</span></span> 

-  <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-106">Hvilke råvarer og hvilken kapacitet er tilgængelig i øjeblikket?</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-106">What raw materials and capacities are currently available?</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-110">De tre vigtigste planlægningsprocesser er:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-110">The three main planning processes are:</span></span>

-  <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-111">**Varedisponering** - Behovsplanen beregner nettobehovene.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-111">**Master planning** - The Master plan calculates net requirements.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-112">Den er baseret på faktiske aktuelle ordrer og gør det muligt for virksomheder at styre genopfyldning af lageret på en kortsigtet daglig basis.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-112">It is based on actual current orders and enables companies to control inventory replenishment on a short-term, day-to-day basis.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-113">Et andet udtryk, der kræver nærmere beskrivelse, er *Plan for nettobehov*.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-113">Another term to describe it is the *Net requirements plan*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-114">Du kan finde flere oplysninger under [Oversigt over Behovsplaner](master-plans.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-114">For more information, see [Master plans overview](master-plans.md).</span></span> 

-  <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-115">**Hovedplanlægning** - Hovedplanen beregner bruttobehov.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-115">**Forecast planning** - The Forecast schedule calculates gross requirements.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-116">Den er baseret på fremtidige projektioner (eller budgetter) og giver firmaer mulighed for udføre langsigtet planlægning for materialer og kapacitet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-116">It is based on future projections (or forecasts), and enables companies to conduct long-term planning of materials and capacity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-117">Du kan finde flere oplysninger under [Oversigt over Behovsprognose](introduction-demand-forecasting.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-117">For more information, see [Demand forecasting overview](introduction-demand-forecasting.md).</span></span> 

-  <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-118">**Intern varedisponering** - Den interne behovsplan beregner nettobehov på tværs af juridiske enheder.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-118">**Intercompany master planning** - The Intercompany master plan calculates net requirements across legal entities.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-119">Den forbinder behov og forsyning mellem firmaer, ikke kun kortsigtet, fast behov og forsyning, men også langsigtet, planlagt (der endnu ikke er autoriseret) behov og forsyning.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-119">It connects demand and supply between companies not only for short term, firm demand and supply but also for long-term, planned (that is not yet firmed) demand and supply.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-120">Du kan finde flere oplysninger i [Intern varedisponering](https://mbspartner.microsoft.com/AX/CourseOverview/1276) (eLearning) (kræver CustomerSource-konto).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-120">For more information, see [Intercompany master planning](https://mbspartner.microsoft.com/AX/CourseOverview/1276)  (eLearning) (requires CustomerSource account).</span></span> 

<span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-121">Firmaer kan ændre resultatet af planen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-121">Companies can change the output of the plan.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-122">De kan køre total, nettoændring eller begge dele.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-122">They can run regenerative, net change, or both.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-123">Totalplaner opdaterer alle behov, mens nettoplaner kun opdaterer planen for varer med nye behov, der er kommet efter den seneste behovsplanlægning.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-123">Regenerative plans update all requirements, whereas, net change plans only update the plan on items with new requirements that have come in since the last scheduling run.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-124">Planer for behovsplanlægning er normalt kortsigtede og kan være alt fra en uge til seks måneder.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-124">Master scheduling plans typically involve the short term, which can be anywhere from one week to six months.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-125">Varedisponeringsmodulet bestemmer forsynings- (materiale) og kapacitetsbehov (ressourcer), som opfylder aktuelle behov (nettobehov).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-125">The Master planning module determines the supply (materials) and capacity (resources) needs that will meet current demand (the net requirements).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="d7a6d-126">I de fleste firmaer omfatter dette også den længste akkumulerede leveringstid for de produkter, der skal modtages.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d7a6d-126">In most companies this is extended to include the longest cumulative lead time among the products to be received.</span></span>
