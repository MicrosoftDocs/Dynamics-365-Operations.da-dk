## <a name="how-master-planning-handles-the-safety-stock-constraint"></a><span data-ttu-id="df30d-208">Sådan håndteres sikkerhedslagerets begrænsning i Varedisponering</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="df30d-208">How master planning handles the safety stock constraint</span></span>
