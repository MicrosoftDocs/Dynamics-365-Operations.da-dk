[!include[task guide banner](../includes/task-guide-banner.md)]

1. <span data-ttu-id="89ab5-107">Klik på Styring af prissætning og rabatter.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="89ab5-107">Click Pricing and discount management.</span></span>

12. <span data-ttu-id="89ab5-119">Indtast en dato i feltet Fra dato.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="89ab5-119">In the From date field, enter a date.</span></span>
13. <span data-ttu-id="89ab5-120">Indtast en dato i feltet Til dato.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="89ab5-120">In the To date field, enter a date.</span></span>

19. <span data-ttu-id="89ab5-129">Indtast et tal i feltet Beløb i valuta.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="89ab5-129">In the Amount in currency field, enter a number.</span></span>

22. <span data-ttu-id="89ab5-132">Luk siden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="89ab5-132">Close the page.</span></span>
23. <span data-ttu-id="89ab5-133">Luk siden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="89ab5-133">Close the page.</span></span>
